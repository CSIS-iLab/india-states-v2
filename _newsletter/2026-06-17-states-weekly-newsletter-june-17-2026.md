**States Weekly Newsletter**

**June 17, 2026**

**Big News: Tamil Nadu** issues **Draft Notification of TNERC (Battery Energy Storage Systems) Regulations, 2026; Uttarakhand** announces the **Uttarakhand Science, Technology and Innovation Policy 2026; Maharashtra** decentralizes **approval processes for government land deals; Haryana** issues a set of **austerity and resource-management guidelines applicable to government departments;** and more.

**Health**

**Andhra Pradesh** launches a pilot initiative to **harness Artificial Intelligence (AI) in detecting diabetic retinopathy**. Under the scheme, images of patients' eyes will be analyzed by AI systems to determine the severity of retinopathy.

**Source:** [New Indian Express](https://www.newindianexpress.com/states/andhra-pradesh/2026/Jun/11/ap-launches-ai-based-pilot-to-detect-diabetic-retinopathy-in-government-hospitals)

**Manipur** launches **13 Mobile Medical Units (MMUs)**. The MMUs will provide a comprehensive range of medical services, health education, awareness programs, and referral services when required.

**Source:** [Imphal Times](https://www.imphaltimes.com/news/cm-flags-off-13-mobile-medical-units/)

**In detail:** [Press Release](https://newsonair.gov.in/manipur-cm-yumnam-khemchand-singh-flags-off-13-mobile-medical-units-for-hill-districts/)

**Climate Change and Energy**

**Delhi** temporarily relaxes **Regulation 172 of the DERC (Terms and Conditions for Determination of Tariff) Regulations, 2017.** The order, issued by the Delhi Electricity Regulatory Commission (DERC), allows DISCOMs to recover a higher Fuel and Power Purchase Adjustment Surcharge (FPPAS) from consumers.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/06/11/derc-allows-higher-power-cost-recovery-for-delhi-discoms-amid-rising-electricity-purchase-expenses/)

**In detail:** [Order](https://www.derc.gov.in/sites/default/files/PPAC_Order.pdf)

**Karnataka** releases **Draft Connectivity and General Network Access to the Intra-State Transmission and State Distribution System Regulations, 2026.** The regulations, released by the Karnataka Electricity Regulatory Commission (KERC) aim to create a more structured and transparent framework for open access and network connectivity across the state.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/06/12/kerc-unveils-draft-2026-regulations-for-open-access-and-general-network-access-in-karnataka/)

**In detail:** [Draft KERC (Connectivity and General Network Accessto the Intra-State Transmission and State Distribution System) Regulations, 2026](https://kerc.karnataka.gov.in/uploads/media_to_upload1781238353.pdf)

**Tamil Nadu** issues **Draft Notification of TNERC (Battery Energy Storage Systems) Regulations, 2026.** The regulations, issued by the Tamil Nadu Electricity Regulatory Commission (TNERC) provide a comprehensive framework for battery energy storage systems in the state.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/tamil-nadu-drafts-new-rules-to-govern-battery-energy-storage-systems)

**In detail:** [Draft Notification of TNERC (Battery Energy Storage Systems) Regulations, 2026](https://tnerc.tn.gov.in/PressRelease/files/PR-150520262110Eng.pdf)

**Meghalaya** issues **Draft Meghalaya State Electricity Regulatory Commission (State Grid Code) Regulations, 2026.** The regulations, issued by the Meghalaya State Electricity Regulatory Commission (MSERC), set out guidelines for the planning, operation, scheduling, and protection of the state power grid.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/meghalaya-power-regulator-issues-draft-grid-code)

**In detail:** [Draft Meghalaya State Electricity Regulatory Commission (State Grid Code)](https://mserc.gov.in/regulations/2026/2_Pre_Publication_MSERC_State_Grid_Code_Regulations_2026.rar)

[Regulations, 2026](https://mserc.gov.in/regulations/2026/2_Pre_Publication_MSERC_State_Grid_Code_Regulations_2026.rar)

**Punjab** approves an **additional surcharge for consumers under the Punjab State Power Corporation (PSPCL)**. The order, issued by the Punjab State Electricity Regulatory Commission (PSERC), allows for an additional surcharge of INR \$0.013 (INR 1.13)/kWh for full open access consumers.

**Source:** [MVA Pulse](https://www.mvapulse.com/punjab-reduces-additional-surcharge-for-open-access-consumers/)

**West Bengal** mandates that all **state government offices move to prepaid smart electricity meters.** The new guidelines also require departments, autonomous bodies, and government-controlled entities to clear outstanding power dues either in a lump sum or over a period of 300 days.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://energy.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/power/west-bengal-government-mandates-smart-prepaid-electric-meters-for-all-state-offices-by-2026/131681841)

**Finance**

Nothing critical to report this week.

**Industrial Policy and Business Regulations**

**Uttarakhand** announces the **Uttarakhand Science, Technology and Innovation Policy 2026.** The policy is designed to provide a common platform for youth, researchers, startups, scientists, and grassroots innovators and encourages the merging of traditional knowledge with modern technology.

**Source:** [The News Mill](https://thenewsmill.com/2026/06/uttarakhand-launches-sti-policy-to-boost-education-healthcare-and-governance/)

**Land Acquisition and Labor Regulations**

**Maharashtra** decentralizes **approval processes for government land deals**. District Collectors and Divisional Commissioners can now approve the sale, transfer, redevelopment, and change of use of government land within certain land value restrictions.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/services/property-/-cstruction/maharashtra-govt-decentralises-land-approval-powers-to-speed-up-projects/articleshow/131623942.cms?from=mdr)

**In detail:** [Order](https://gr.maharashtra.gov.in/Site/Upload/Government%20Resolutions/English/202606051627553519.pdf)

**Infrastructure and Governance**

**Bihar** mandates **approvals for all investment proposals to be issued within 30 days**. If no decision is taken by the authorities concerned within a 30-day time limit, "deemed clearance" will automatically be granted.

**Source:** [PSU Watch](https://psuwatch.com/amp/story/newsupdates/investment-proposals-in-bihar-to-be-given-approvals-within-30-days-state-govt)

**In detail:** [Press Release](https://cm.bihar.gov.in/users/downloadrelease.aspx?id=11202) (Hindi)

**Delhi** introduces an **incentive scheme for its employees who commute using the metro or other public transport services**. Under the scheme, employees opting to use a common mobility card issued by the Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) will be eligible for an additional incentive equivalent to 10 percent of their admissible transport allowance.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/delhi/delhi-govt-offers-10-extra-transport-allowance-to-staff-using-public-transport/articleshow/131615998.cms)

**Haryana** allows families residing on **village 'shamlat' land (common-use land) for over 20 years to apply for ownership rights**. The applications for ownership transfers will be open until January 16, 2027.

Source: [Millennium Post](https://www.millenniumpost.in/amp/nation/haryana-to-strengthen-village-sanitation-governance-saini-663620)

In detail: [Press Release](https://www.haryanacmoffice.gov.in/cmo-haryana-press-release-9-june-2026-0)

**Madhya Pradesh** withdraws **the two-child restriction linked to government service in the draft Madhya Pradesh Civil Services Rules.** The rule prescribed a maximum limit of two children for eligibility for government employment.

**Source**: [New Indian Express](https://www.newindianexpress.com/states/madhya-pradesh/2026/Jun/10/madhya-pradesh-cm-directs-withdrawal-of-two-child-restriction-for-government-service)

**Tamil Nadu** launches the '**Singappen' (Lioness) Special Force (SSF).** The SSF will operate as a specialized police unit for women's safety.

**Source:** [The News Minute](https://www.thenewsminute.com/tamil-nadu/tn-cm-vijay-launches-singappen-special-force-allots-rs-354-crores)

**In detail:** [Press Release](https://cms.tn.gov.in/cms_migrated/document/press_release/pr090626_cm1.jpg)

**Uttar Pradesh** launches **Ganga (Government Assisted Network for Growth and Advancement).** The scheme aims to provide high-speed fiber-based broadband connectivity to thousands of rural households across Uttar Pradesh.

**Source:** [Hindustan Times](https://www.hindustantimes.com/cities/lucknow-news/project-ganga-up-govt-unveils-massive-rural-broadband-push-101781017668481.html)

**Assam** restricts issuance of **new Aadhaar cards for individuals above 18 years of age.** The rule does not apply to Scheduled Tribes, Schedules Castes, and Tea Garden communities until March 2027.

**Source:** [Asianet News](https://newsable.asianetnews.com/india/assam-cabinet-restricts-aadhaar-approves-guwahati-satellite-city-plan-articleshow-4qkkvoc)

**In detail:** [Cabinet Decision](https://cm.assam.gov.in/cabinet-decisions-details?articleId=918359)

**Kerala** approves a **free bus travel scheme for women and transgender individuals.** The new rules will apply on "ordinary services" of the Kerala State Road Transport Corporation's (KSRTC) buses from June 15, 2026 onwards.

**Source:** [The Statesman](https://www.thestatesman.com/india/kerala-cabinet-clears-free-travel-concession-for-women-on-ksrtcs-ordinary-services-from-june-15-1503604271.html)

**In detail:** [Notification](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:4994c35c-ae33-49a1-b83c-9c2f98c8c348)

**Meghalaya** launches a **One-Time Settlement (OTS) scheme aimed at providing relief to commercial vehicle owners by waiving outstanding taxes and penalties.** Commercial vehicle owners will receive an 80 percent waiver on tax dues and penalties, and a 90 percent waiver on penalties related to their vehicle's Fitness Certificates.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/guwahati/ots-scheme-for-commercial-vehicles-launched-in-meghalaya/articleshow/131666945.cms)

**In detail:** [Press Release](https://meghalaya.gov.in/press/content/51012)

**Chhattisgarh** approves the transfer of **yoga-related functions from the Social Welfare Department to the Medical Education Department.** The decision will integrate yoga with healthcare and improve training, research, and academic coordination in the field of wellness and traditional medicinal systems.

**Source:** [Indian Masterminds](https://indianmasterminds.com/news/chhattisgarh-cabinet-approves-e-buses-csptcl-ipo-krishak-unnati-yojana-209338/)

**In detail:** [Cabinet Decision](https://dprcg.gov.in/pages/1768982672/decisions-of-the-council-of-ministers-2026)

**Odisha** announces that **education in government schools and colleges will be provided free of cost from kindergarten to postgraduate level.** The policy intends to make education universally accessible.

**Source:** [Telegraph India](https://www.telegraphindia.com/edugraph/news/odisha-govt-announces-free-education-from-kg-to-pg-in-government-schools-and-colleges/cid/2165374#goog_rewarded)

**Odisha** publishes **Operational Guidelines for Promotion of Heritage Properties.** The framework introduces incentives and capital subsidies for property owners who restore and repurpose historical structures built before 1950.

**Source:** [Odisha Bytes](https://odishabytes.com/odisha-rolls-out-guidelines-to-turn-pre-1950-heritage-properties-into-elite-tourism-hubs/)

**Karnataka** announces **free bus passes for students attending schools and colleges within the state.**

**Source:** [ETV Bharat](https://www.etvbharat.com/en/state/karnataka-govt-rolls-out-free-bus-scheme-for-all-students-extends-to-outside-state-enn26061206486)

**In detail:** [Notification](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:46ad6a74-453f-4876-8aa9-7f5609030115)

**Any Other**

**Himachal Pradesh** decides to grant **full salary to employees pursuing higher education while on approved study leave.** Previously, employees on approved study leave would only be granted a portion of their regular pay.

**Source:** [HR Katha](https://www.hrkatha.com/news/himachal-pradesh-govt-approves-full-salary-for-employees-on-study-leave/)

**In detail:** [Press Release](http://himachalpr.gov.in/OnePressRelease.aspx?Language=1&ID=47496)

**Bihar** disallows **government schoolteachers from teaching at coaching centers, private tuition classes and other commercial educational institutions.**

**Source:** [India TV News](https://www.indiatvnews.com/bihar/news-bihar-issues-new-guidelines-for-govt-teachers-warns-disciplinary-action-against-violators-2026-06-11-1044525)

**Haryana** issues a set of **austerity and resource-management guidelines applicable to government departments.** The measures, which come into immediate effect and will remain operational till September 2026 include restrictions on foreign travel, virtual meetings when possible, reduction in fuel consumption targets, energy-saving measures in government buildings, and more.

Source: [Tribune](https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/haryana/curbs-on-foreign-travel-cut-in-petrol-expenses-more-haryana-govts-austerity-measures/)

In detail: [Press Release](https://prharyana.gov.in/en/press-notes-230)
