**States Weekly Newsletter**

**February 4, 2026**

**Big News: Tamil Nadu** releases a **new Integrated Textile Policy; Rajasthan** announces the **Rajasthan Aerospace and Defense Policy; Gujarat **announces that **land for essential civic facilities will now be allocated to municipalities free of charge; Uttar Pradesh **approves the **Uttar Pradesh Urban Redevelopment Policy 2026; Uttarakhand **approves a** Green Hydrogen Policy 2026; **and more. 

**Health **

**Karnataka **mandates** paid menstrual leave. **The** **paid menstrual leave applies for women government employees working under the Education Department. 

**Source:** [BW people](https://www.bwpeople.in/article/karnataka-education-department-formalises-paid-menstrual-leave-policy-591542)

**In detail:** [Government order](https://finance.karnataka.gov.in/uploads/media_to_upload1764762820.pdf) (in Kannada)

**Uttar Pradesh **announces** a cashless medical treatment facility for teacher**s. The new initiative offers cashless medical treatment to teachers, non-teaching staff, and their dependents.

**Source:** [CSR Journal](https://thecsrjournal.in/uttar-pradesh-cabinet-approves-cashless-medical-cover/)

**In detail:** [Cabinet decisions](https://information.up.gov.in/admin/UploadDocument/OtherPress/compressed_30012026124433PN-CM-Cabinet%20Decisions-29%20January,%202026.pdf)

**Climate Change and Energy **

**Rajasthan** issues** (RERC)** **Draft Procedure for Grant of Connectivity to the Intra-State Transmission System**. The procedures issued by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) approve a new procedure for granting connectivity to the state’s intra-state transmission system. 

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](The%20Rajasthan%20Electricity%20Regulatory%20Commission%20has%20issued%20an%20important%20order%20approving%20a%20new%20procedure%20for%20granting%20connectivity%20to%20the%20state’s%20intra-state%20transmission%20system.%20This%20decision%20comes%20after%20the%20notification%20of%20the%20Rajasthan%20Electricity%20Grid%20Code%20Regulations%20in%20early%202025,%20which%20directed%20the%20State%20Transmission%20Utility,%20Rajasthan%20Rajya%20Vidyut%20Prasaran%20Nigam,%20to%20submit%20revised%20procedures%20for%20providing%20grid%20connectivity%20in%20the%20state.)

**In detail:** [Draft Procedure for Grant of Connectivity to the Intra-State Transmission System](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:6e4c6d27-3ad1-442c-996c-99e62b7f2e56) 

**Rajasthan** introduces **dual-source electricity supply for high-tension (HT) and extra-high tension (EHT) consumers.** The order issued by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) applies to the Public Health Engineering Department (PHED), data centers, and other essential service categories. 

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/rajasthan-regulator-allows-dual-power-connections-for-high-load-consumers)

**In detail:** [RERC Order](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:872c6ee5-5cab-492f-8291-c0d337865b4c)

**Rajasthan** issues **RERC Draft Regulations on Demand Flexibility (DF), Demand Side Management (DSM), 2026**. The regulations issued by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) propose a comprehensive regulatory framework to integrate demand-side resources into the state’s power system.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/rajasthan-regulator-proposes-demand-flexibility-dsm-regulations)

**In detail:** [Draft (RERC) (Demand Flexibility (DF), Demand Side Management (DSM)) Regulations, 2026](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:7e3cfc16-459c-4918-8f43-f8f01de5b7d2) 

**Telangana **notifies **(TERC)** **First Amendment to the Terms and Conditions of Open Access Regulation, 2024.** The amendments, issued by the Telangana Electricity Regulatory Commission (TERC) introduces changes related to the issuance of Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs) for unutilized banked renewable energy. 

**Source: **[**Saur Energy**](https://www.saurenergy.com/solar-energy-news/telangana-updates-open-access-regulations-to-clarify-rec-treatment-11033774)

**In detail:** [TERC (Terms and conditions of Open Access) First Amendment regulation, 2026](https://www.tgerc.telangana.gov.in/file_upload/uploads/Regulations/Final/tserc/2026/1st%20Amend%20to%20Reg%201%20of%202024%20-%20Final%2024012026.pdf)

**Chhattisgarh **clarifies its **2022 Guidelines for Group Net Metering (GNM) and Virtual Net Metering (VNM) for Renewable Energy**. The clarification aims to provide clear directions to both the utility and consumers, ensuring proper implementation of renewable energy policies in the state.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/01/27/cserc-clarifies-group-and-virtual-net-metering-rules-boosting-renewable-energy-adoption-in-chhattisgarh/)

**In detail:** [CSERC order](https://cserc.gov.in/upload/order/O-133-2025_9595-1769143706-.pdf)

**Odisha **introduces **OERG Grid Support Charges for Captive Generating Plants Regulations, 2026. **The regulations issued by the Odisha Electricity Regulatory Commission** **aim to create a clear and technically justified mechanism for recovering the cost of grid support services provided to conventional captive power plants in Odisha.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/01/27/oerc-notifies-grid-support-charges-regulations-2026-for-captive-power-plants-in-odisha/)

**In detail: **[OERC GSC Regulations 2026](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:30e972c2-2d7d-47b7-ad1b-119230730a5d)

**Industrial Policy and Business Regulations  **

**Rajasthan** announces the **Rajasthan Aerospace and Defense Policy**. The policy aims to promote manufacturing and services in the aerospace and defense (A&D) sector and increase the number of aerospace and defense manufacturing units and repair and overhaul (MRO) facilities in the state.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://government.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/policy/rajasthan-cm-bhajanlal-sharma-unveils-aerospace-defence-policy/127775996)

**In detail: **[Cabinet decisions](https://cmo.rajasthan.gov.in/programdetail/232508)

**Rajasthan** amends **The Rajasthan Excise and Temperance Policy 2025–2029**. The policy paves the way for extended operating hours for liquor shops across the state. 

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/jaipur/liquor-shops-may-remain-opentill-10pm-retail-prices-set-to-rise/articleshow/127729214.cms)

**In detail:** [Amendment in Excise and Temperance Policy 2025-2029](https://finance.rajasthan.gov.in/PDFDOCS/EXCISE/14808.pdf)

**Tamil Nadu** releases a new **Integrated Textile Policy. **The policy aims to address the current needs of the textile industry by supporting modernization, competitiveness, and local manufacturing.

**Source: **[The Hindu](https://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/Coimbatore/textile-policy-aims-to-meet-the-current-needs-of-the-industry/article70570666.ece)

**In detail**: [Press release](https://cms.tn.gov.in/cms_migrated/document/press_release/pr290126_230.pdf)

**Land Acquisition and Labor Regulations  **

**Gujarat **announces that **land for essential civic facilities will now be allocated to municipalities free of charge**. By waiving these costs, the government aims to drastically reduce the financial burden on 152 municipalities, ensuring that budget constraints no longer stall critical public works.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/ahmedabad/gujarat-government-to-allot-land-for-free-to-municipalities-for-public-works/articleshow/127647493.cms)

**In detail:** [Press release](https://cmogujarat.gov.in/en/important-decisions/cm-shri-bhupendra-patel%E2%80%99s-important-decision-to-allot-government-land-free-of-cost-to-municipalities-for-11-types-of-basic-infrastructure-facilities)

**Infrastructure and Governance**

**Bihar** approves **financial assistance to women entrepreneurs**. The state will give up to $2,180 (INR 2 lakh) to women entrepreneurs under the ‘Mukhyamantri Mahila Rozgar Yojana’ (Chief Minister’s Women Employment Scheme).

**Source: **[The Hindu](https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/bihar/bihar-cabinet-approves-additional-financial-assistance-to-beneficiaries-of-mukhyamantri-mahila-rozgar-yojana/article70566954.ece)

**In detail:** [Cabinet decisions](https://state.bihar.gov.in/cache/1/Smart%20City/Cabinet%20Decisions/dd29012026.pdf)

**Madhya Pradesh **approves the **Overseas Employment Placement Scheme for OBC Youth 2025. ** Under the new scheme an allocation of $4.95 million (INR 45 crore) has been approved, and the state will facilitate foreign employment opportunities for 600 OBC youth every year over the next three years.  

**Source:** [Millennium post](https://www.millenniumpost.in/nation/madhya-pradesh-cabinet-approves-overseas-job-scheme-for-obc-youth-645876)

**In detail:** [Cabinet decisions](https://www.mpinfo.org/Home/CabinetDetails?newsid=%C2%B2%C2%B0%C2%B2%C2%B6%C2%B0%C2%B1%C2%B2%C2%B7%C3%8E%C2%B2%C2%B1%C2%B1&fontname=%C3%8D%C3%A1%C3%AE%C3%A7%C3%A1%C3%AC&LocID=%C2%B3%C2%B2&pubdate=01/27/2026)

**Uttar Pradesh **approves the **Uttar Pradesh Urban Redevelopment Policy 2026.** The policy aims to provide for the redevelopment of city areas and buildings that were once developed but are now underutilized

**Source: **[Hindustan Times](https://www.hindustantimes.com/cities/lucknow-news/uttar-pradesh-cabinet-decision-urban-redevelopment-policy-gets-approval-101769716486638.html)

**In detail:** [Cabinet decisions](https://information.up.gov.in/admin/UploadDocument/OtherPress/compressed_30012026124433PN-CM-Cabinet%20Decisions-29%20January,%202026.pdf)

**Arunachal Pradesh **approves the **Arunachal Pradesh Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Bill, 2026. **The bill decriminalizes minor offences and replaces imprisonment with monetary penalties. 

**Source:** [India Today](https://www.indiatodayne.in/arunachal-pradesh/story/arunachal-cabinet-clears-hydropower-incentives-recruitment-reforms-and-governance-overhaul-1339280-2026-01-31)

**Odisha **approves the **State Cooperation Policy 2026.** The policy framework seeks to revitalize the state’s cooperative movement by encouraging member participation, financial inclusion, and digital service delivery. 

**Source:** [New Indian Express](https://www.newindianexpress.com/states/odisha/2026/Feb/01/cooperation-policy-gets-odisha-govt-cabinet-nod-to-push-financial-inclusion)

**Uttarakhand **approves the** Health Worker and Health Supervisor Service Rules 2026. **The rules streamline employment and service standards for health personnel.

**Source:** [Indian masterminds](https://indianmasterminds.com/news/uttarakhand-cabinet-approvals-health-education-green-hydrogen-industry-180503/)

**Uttarakhand **approves a** Green Hydrogen Policy 2026. **The policy aims to promote green hydrogen production and sustainable energy initiatives in the state.

**Source:** [Indian masterminds](https://indianmasterminds.com/news/uttarakhand-cabinet-approvals-health-education-green-hydrogen-industry-180503/)
