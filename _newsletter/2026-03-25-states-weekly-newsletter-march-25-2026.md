**States Weekly Newsletter**

**March 25, 2026**

**Big News: Maharashtra** passes the **Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Bill, 2026; Haryana** passes the **Haryana Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (Amendment) Bill, 2026; Maharashtra** passes the **Maharashtra Repealing Bill, 2026; Karnataka** passes the **Karnataka Municipalities and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2026;** and more.

**Health** 

**Haryana** announces Phase II of the **Nirogi Haryana Program**. The program aims to ensure one comprehensive health screening for eligible citizens every two years. Under Phase II, the annual family income eligibility limit has increased from $1,918 to $3,197 (INR 1.8 lakh to 3 lakh), which is expected to increase the size of the program.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/gurgaon/haryana-govt-raises-health-scheme-income-cap-from-rs-1-8-lakh-to-rs-3-lakh/articleshow/129617686.cms)

**Mizoram** launches the **Mizoram Universal Health Care Scheme 2.0 (MUHCS 2.0).** The revamped scheme will come into effect from April 1, 2026, introducing stricter emergency protocols and enhanced financial provisions to ensure long-term sustainability.

**Source:** [India Today](https://www.indiatodayne.in/mizoram/story/mizoram-launches-upgraded-universal-health-care-scheme-with-stricter-emergency-norms-1360829-2026-03-17)

**In detail:** [Press release](https://dipr.mizoram.gov.in/post/chief-minister-launches-mizoram-universal-health-care-scheme-201773656176)

**Climate Change and Energy**

**Gujarat** reduces the **additional surcharge for Open Access Consumers**. The order, issued by the Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC), reduced the surcharge by ~$0.002 (INR 0.24)/kWh to ~$0.008 (INR 0.76)/kWh, from April 1, 2026, to September 30, 2026.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/gujarat-cuts-additional-surcharge-for-open-access-power-to-%E2%82%B90-76-kwh)

**In detail:** [GERC order](https://gercin.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Order-Addl.-Surcharge-payable-by-OA-consumers-for-the-period-01.04.26-to-30.09.26-dtd.-17.03.2026.pdf)

**Maharashtra** passes the **Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Bill, 2026.** The bill aims to curb air pollution by increasing the environmental tax on old and polluting vehicles.

**Source:** [NDTV](https://www.ndtvprofit.com/india/maharashtra-legislative-assembly-unanimously-passes-amendment-bill-to-curb-rising-air-pollution-increase-environment-tax-on-old-vehicles-11234896)

**In detail:** [Maharashtra Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Act, 2026](https://prsindia.org/files/bills_acts/bills_states/maharashtra/2026/Bill23of2026MH.pdf)

**Maharashtra** announces a **Renewable Energy and Energy Storage Policy for 2025-26 to 2035-36.** The policy aims to significantly scale up clean energy adoption while ensuring grid stability through energy storage systems, reflecting both rising electricity demand and climate commitments.

**Source:** [Business World](https://www.businessworld.in/article/inside-maharashtras-big-bet-on-renewable-energy-and-storage-598214)

**In detail:** [Maharashtra Renewable Energy and Energy Storage Policy for 2025-26 to 2035-36](https://gr.maharashtra.gov.in/Site/Upload/Government%20Resolutions/English/202603181848365810.pdf)

**Rajasthan** releases **draft RERC (Terms and Conditions for Tariff Determination from Renewable Energy Sources) (Third Amendment) Regulations, 2026.** The regulations, issued by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) propose to extend the validity of the current Renewal Energy Tariff Regulations until March 31, 2028, while introducing changes affecting biomass power projects, energy storage systems, payment discipline, and open access provisions.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/rajasthan-regulator-proposes-changes-to-renewable-energy-tariff-regulations)

**In detail:** [RERC (Terms and Conditions for Tariff determination from Renewable Energy Sources) (Third Amendment) Regulations, 2026](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:3384be34-5693-4bb3-a008-279f9309fa37)

**Rajasthan** issues **RERC (Battery Energy Storage Systems) Regulations, 2026.** The regulations, published by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) focus on promoting the use of Battery Energy Storage Systems across the entire power value chain, including generation, transmission, and distribution.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/03/17/rajasthan-introduces-bess-regulations-2026-to-boost-energy-storage-and-grid-stability/)

**In detail:** [RERC (Battery Energy Storage Systems) Regulations, 2026](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:71a7adb2-9e3d-422a-a715-4c07f157dcf3)

**Himachal Pradesh** fixes the rate of sale of **Free Power of the Government of** **Himachal Pradesh to Himachal Pradesh State Electricity Board Limited (HPSEBL) for FY 2026-27.** The Himachal Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (HPERC) has set the rate at ~$0.0292 (INR 2.73)/kWh for the financial year, marking an increase from ~$0.0289 (2.70)/kWh in the previous year.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/hperc-fixes-himachals-free-power-rate-at-%E2%82%B92-73-kwh-for-fy-2027)

**In detail:** [HPERC order](https://hperc.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/freepower26-27.pdf)

**Punjab** amends **PSERC (Electricity Supply Code, Standard of Performance and Related Matters) (1st Amendment) Regulations, 2026.** The amendments, issued by the Punjab State Electricity Regulatory Commission (PSERC), allow for the release of electricity connections in “abandoned colonies” where builders have failed to complete infrastructure.

**Source:** [Tribune](https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/jalandhar/punjab-state-electricity-regulatory-commission-eases-norms-for-electricity-connections-in-abandoned-stalled-housing-projects-across-state/)

**In detail:** [PSERC (Electricity Supply Code, Standard of Performance and related matters) (1st Amendment) Regulations, 2026](https://docs.pspcl.in/docs/cecommercial2620260320182957438.pdf)

**Punjab** releases **draft PSERC (Terms and Conditions for Intra-State Open Access) (13th Amendment) Regulations, 2026.** The regulations issued by the Punjab State Electricity Regulatory Commission (PSERC) have a key focus on the treatment of banked energy for green energy consumers and renewable energy-based captive power users. Under the proposed amendment, banked energy can now be carried forward on a month-to-month basis until the end of the settlement period, which runs from June 1 to May 31 of the following year.

**Source:** [Energetica](https://www.energetica-india.net/news/punjab-proposes-to-allow-banked-energy-carry-forward-in-intra-state-open-access-projects)

**In detail:** [PSERC (Terms and Conditions for intra-State Open Access) (13th Amendment) Regulations, 2026](https://pserc.punjab.gov.in/pages/Amendment-Regulation28%286%29ofOA.pdf)

**Industrial Policy and Business Regulations**  

**Delhi** extends its **2025-26** **Excise Policy for one year**. The renewal and issuances of new retail liquor licenses for 2026-27 will be made on the same terms and conditions as the existing policy.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://hospitality.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/operations/food-and-beverages/delhi-government-extends-retail-liquor-licenses-for-2026-27-amid-new-policy-development/129668910)

**In detail:** [Government notification](https://excise.delhi.gov.in/sites/default/files/Excise/circulars-orders/order_retail_licenses.pdf)

**Haryana** announces that **registered vehicle scrapping and recycling units in the state will be granted industry status.** Under the capital subsidy scheme, both new industrial units and units undertaking expansion will get subsidy on capital investment at prescribed rates.

**Source:** [Hindustan Times](https://www.hindustantimes.com/cities/chandigarh-news/hry-announces-industry-status-for-registered-vehicle-recycling-units-101773411255349.html)

**In detail:** [Press Release](https://haryanacmoffice.gov.in/cmo-haryana-press-release-13-march-2026-0)

**Infrastructure and Governance**

**Andhra Pradesh** launches the ‘**Divyang Shakti’ (Power to the Disabled) Scheme.** The scheme provides free bus travel to people with disabilities.

**Source:** [Telangana today](https://telanganatoday.com/andhra-pradesh-launches-divyang-shakti-scheme-offering-free-bus-travel-for-people-with-disabilities)

**Haryana** passes the **Haryana Development and Regulation of Urban Areas (Amendment) Bill, 2026.** The bill amends Section 7-A of the Act of 1975, bringing exchange deeds within a regulatory ambit. Section 7-A required a No Objection Certificate (NoC) to register transactions involving small urban land parcels to prevent unauthorized development, but parties circumvented this by using exchange deeds to disguise sales.

**Source:** [Hindustan Times](https://www.hindustantimes.com/cities/chandigarh-news/5-bills-cleared-haryana-assembly-passes-bill-to-plug-loopholes-in-urban-area-law-101773863034401.html)

**In detail:** [Haryana Development and Regulation of Urban Areas amendment Bill, 2026](https://haryanaassembly.gov.in/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Bill-No.-6-HLA-of-2026.pdf)

**Haryana** passes the **Haryana Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (Amendment) Bill, 2026.** The bill caps incremental guarantees on term loan to be given during a year at 5 percent of revenue receipts of the previous year or 0.5 percent of Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) of the previous year, whichever is lower.

**Source:** [Hindustan Times](https://www.hindustantimes.com/cities/chandigarh-news/5-bills-cleared-haryana-assembly-passes-bill-to-plug-loopholes-in-urban-area-law-101773863034401.html)

**In detail:** [Fiscal Responsibility Bill](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:45a3884a-eec1-46b6-b984-97990b02f8d8)

**Maharashtra** passes **The Maharashtra Self-Financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) Amendment Bill, 2026. T**he Bill permits institutions operating at the national level to establish self-financed schools in Maharashtra, whereas earlier only state-registered bodies were permitted.

**Source:** [Edex](https://www.edexlive.com/news/maharashtra-assembly-clears-bill-to-strengthen-regulation-of-self-financed-schools)

**In detail:** [Maharashtra Self-financed Schools (Establishment and Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 2026](https://prsindia.org/files/bills_acts/bills_states/maharashtra/2026/Bill16of2026MH.pdf)

**Maharashtra** passes the **Maharashtra Freedom of Religion Bill, 2026**. The bill prohibits religious conversions carried out through coercion, fraud, inducement, or marriage.

**Source:** [News on AIR](https://www.newsonair.gov.in/maharashtra-assembly-passes-freedom-of-religion-bill-2026-to-curb-forced-conversions/)

**In detail:** [Maharashtra Freedom of Religion Act, 2026](https://prsindia.org/files/bills_acts/bills_states/maharashtra/2026/Bill20of2026MH.pdf)

**Maharashtra** passes the **Maharashtra Repealing Bill, 2026.** The bill repeals around 80 outdated and obsolete laws in the state.

**Source:** [NDTV](https://www.ndtv.com/india-news/maharashtra-assembly-passes-bill-to-repeal-80-obsolete-laws-11229034)

**In detail:** [Maharashtra Repealing Act, 2026](https://prsindia.org/files/bills_acts/bills_states/maharashtra/2026/Bill17of2026MH.pdf)

**Karnataka** passes the **Karnataka Municipalities and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2026.** The bill aims at regulating outdoor advertisements and enabling municipal councils and corporations to levy an advertisement fee on any person who erects, exhibits, fixes or displays an advertisement on land, buildings, walls, hoardings or other structures within municipal limits.

**Source:** [Deccan Chronicle](https://www.deccanchronicle.com/southern-states/karnataka/karnataka-assembly-passes-bill-to-regulate-hoardings-levy-advertisement-fee-1944220)

**In detail:** [Karnataka Municipalities and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Bill, 2026](https://prsindia.org/files/bills_acts/bills_states/karnataka/2026/Bill5of2026KA.pdf)
