**States Weekly Newsletter**

**July 1, 2026**

**Big News: West Bengal** amends the **West Bengal Shops and Establishment Act, 1963; Chhattisgarh** approves the **Chhattisgarh Compressed Biogas (CG-CBG) Policy 2026; Maharashtra** approves draft **Maharashtra Women Farmers Empowerment Bill 2026; Assam** approves the **Assam Tourism Accommodation (Development and Registration) Rules 2026; Andhra Pradesh** approves a **Policy on Reuse of Treated Used Water, 2026;** and more.

**Health**

**Delhi** approves a **health scheme for registered construction workers and their family members.** The scheme will offer cashless medical treatment cover of up to \$10,584 (INR 10 lakh).

**Source:** [India Today](https://www.indiatoday.in/cities/delhi/story/delhi-construction-workers-health-scheme-rs-10-lakh-cashless-treatment-ptag-2932827-2026-06-23)

**Maharashtra** prohibits the **use of** **newspapers and recycled printed paper for serving, wrapping, and storing food items.** The policy aims to prevent harmful chemicals present in printing ink and recycled paper from contaminating food items.

**Source:** [Organiser](https://organiser.org/2026/06/26/359834/bharat/food-safety-first-maharashtra-govt-bans-newspapers-and-recycled-printed-paper-for-food-packaging-over-health-concerns/)

**Climate Change and Energy**

**Haryana** amends the **Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) of the Solar Energy Promotion Scheme.** The revised scheme adds graded interest-free assistance to the central subsidy and relaxes eligibility: applicants now need only be non-defaulters in the previous billing cycle, down from three years of regular payments.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://energy.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/renewable/haryana-targets-2-lakh-rooftop-solar-installations-by-2027-eases-norms/131981437)

**In detail:** [SOP Notification](https://uhbvn.org.in/staticContent/documents/circular/SC2026/SC_U_02_2026.pdf)

**Haryana** announces **incentives to replace old commercial vehicles.** Among other incentives, Haryana notifies a 100 percent exemption from Motor Vehicle Tax (MVT) on the purchase of new Bharat Stage (BS)-VI or higher emission standard vehicles, Electric Vehicles (EVs) and Compressed Natural Gas (CNG)-run vehicles.

**Source:** [Tribune](https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/haryana/haryana-govt-rolls-out-incentives-to-replace-1-08-lakh-old-trucks-buses-in-ncr/)

**In detail:** [Order](https://haryanatransport.gov.in/sites/default/files/Gazette_98-2026_Ext_30739.pdf)

**Karnataka** notifies the **KERC Forecasting, Scheduling and Deviation Settlement Mechanism (DSM) Regulations, 2026.** The regulations, published by the Karnataka Electricity Regulatory Commission (KERC), introduce a new framework for wind, solar, and wind-solar hybrid energy projects across the state.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/06/24/kerc-notifies-dsm-regulations-2026-tightens-forecasting-norms-for-solar-wind-and-hybrid-projects-in-karnataka/)

**In detail:** [KERC Forecasting, Scheduling and Deviation Settlement Mechanism (DSM) Regulations, 2026](https://kerc.karnataka.gov.in/uploads/media_to_upload1782209789.pdf)

**Uttar Pradesh** implements the change in the **Goods and Services Tax (GST) on renewable energy devices and components from 12 percent to 5 percent for projects implemented under the PM-Kusum Component-C2.** The order was issued by the Uttar Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (UPERC).

**Source:** [Saur Energy](https://www.saurenergy.com/solar-energy-news/uperc-declares-gst-cut-on-renewable-equipment-a-change-in-law-event-for-pm-kusum-projects-12067119)

**In detail:** [UPERC order](https://www.uperc.org/App_File/75SM0f2026-PDF623202664835PM.pdf)

**Chhattisgarh** approves the **Chhattisgarh Compressed Biogas (CG-CBG) Policy 2026.** The policy promotes the production of compressed biogas from agricultural residue, cattle dung, and other organic waste.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://energy.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/oil-and-gas/chhattisgarh-govt-approves-compressed-biogas-policy-2026/131960735)

**Jharkhand** approves **JSERC (Terms and Conditions for Determination of Distribution Tariff) Regulations, 2025.** The amendment, passed by the Jharkhand State Electricity Regulatory Commission (JSERC) introduces a new provision which changes the treatment of power cost reductions in cases where distribution companies have outstanding regulatory assets or approved financial gaps.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/06/23/jserc-amends-tariff-rules-to-prioritize-discom-financial-gap-recovery-over-immediate-consumer-benefits-in-jharkhand/)

**Finance**

**Himachal Pradesh** launches the **Agriculture Loan Interest Subvention Scheme**. The scheme would aid farmers whose agricultural land is at risk of auction due to loan defaults. Under this scheme, the government would bear 50 percent of the outstanding interest liability on agricultural loans up to \$3,180 (INR 3 lakh) per farmer.

**Source:** [Money Control](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/india/himachal-pradesh-govt-to-bear-50-percent-interest-burden-on-overdue-agriculture-loans-13960179.html)

**In detail:** [Press release](http://himachalpr.gov.in/OnePressRelease.aspx?Language=1&ID=47736)

**Industrial Policy and Business Regulations**

**Bihar** approves the **Bihar Sugar Cane Industries Investment Incentive Policy 2026**. As per the policy, investors will get 100 percent reimbursement of registration and stamp duty on land purchases and full reimbursement of State Goods and Services Tax (S-GST) on sugar production for 5 years.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/patna/govt-approves-sugar-cane-industry-incentive-policy/amp_articleshow/131998243.cms)

**Delhi** brings **23 new services under the Delhi Right of Citizens to Time-Bound Delivery of Services Act, 2011**. The services cover licenses, No Objection Certificates, registrations, and approvals across departments, including Labor, Tourism, Jal (Water) Board, Energy, Excise, and Forests, with timelines ranging from a single working day to 60 days.

**Source:** [Daily Pioneer](https://dailypioneer.com/news/govt-brings-23-new-services-under-time-bound-delivery-act)

**Land Acquisition and Labor Regulations**

**Assam** approves the **automatic conversion of agricultural land for setting up micro enterprises, khadi units, and solar power projects.**

**Source:** [Travel trend](https://www.traveltrendstoday.in/assam-boosts-ease-of-doing-business-in-tourism-with-single-window-registration-auto-renewals)

**In detail:** [Cabinet Decisions](https://cm.assam.gov.in/cabinet-decisions-details?articleId=924301)

**West Bengal** amends the **West Bengal Shops and Establishment Act, 1963, to permit businesses to operate on a 24×7 basis.**

**Source:** [The Statesman](https://www.thestatesman.com/cities/kolkata/land-bank-24x7-ops-for-shops-startup-policy-bengal-budget-2026-unveils-major-reforms-to-boost-investment-1503608821.html)

**In detail:** [Budget Speech 2026-27](https://finance.wb.gov.in/writereaddata/Budget_Speech/2026_English.pdf)

**Haryana** approves the **Model Online Transfer Policy (MOTP), 2026 and the Teachers Transfer Policy (TTP), 2026.** The policies aim to make the transfer of government employees and teachers more transparent, systematic, and merit-based through a standardized online process, reducing discretion and streamlining postings across departments and schools.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://auto.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/industry/haryana-cabinet-unveils-major-reforms-new-transfer-policy-and-vehicle-incentives/131925439)

**In detail:** [Cabinet Decisions](https://prharyana.gov.in/en/news-press-releases)

**Infrastructure and Governance**

**Madhya Pradesh** approves **new terms and conditions for providing short-term crop loans to farmers at zero percent interest during the 2026-27 financial year.** Under the revised system, farmers will no longer have separate loan repayment deadlines for the 'Kharif' (monsoon) and 'Rabi' (winter) seasons. Instead, they will receive a single annual credit limit, with separate sub-limits for cash withdrawals and input purchases.

**Source:** [Rural Voice](https://eng.ruralvoice.in/state/madhya-pradesh-cabinet-approves-revised-terms-for-interest-free-crop-loans-to-farmers.html)

**In detail:** [Cabinet Decisions](https://www.mpinfo.org/Home/CabinetDetails?newsid=%C2%B2%C2%B0%C2%B2%C2%B6%C2%B0%C2%B6%C2%B2%C2%B3%C3%8E%C2%B2%C2%B8&fontname=%C3%8D%C3%A1%C3%AE%C3%A7%C3%A1%C3%AC&LocID=%C2%B3%C2%B2&pubdate=06/23/2026)

**Maharashtra** approves draft **Maharashtra Women Farmers Empowerment Bill 2026**. The bill legally recognizes women farmers, regardless of land ownership. The recognition will enable them to access government welfare schemes and subsidies.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/india/maharashtra-cabinet-clears-draft-bill-to-recognise-women-farmers-widen-welfare-access/articleshow/132010214.cms)

**Maharashtra** issues a **Comprehensive Food Safety Compliance Order**. The order mandates that all restaurants, hotels, and similar establishments must provide free drinking water to customers.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://hospitality.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/operations/food-and-beverages/hotels-restaurants-must-provide-free-water-says-maharashtra-fda-issues-comprehensive-order-on-food-safety/131983342)

**Assam** approves the **Assam Tourism Accommodation (Development and Registration) Rules 2026.** The new rules introduce a single-window registration system for hotels, resorts, guest houses, homestays, service apartments, and heritage properties.

Source: [Travel trend](https://www.traveltrendstoday.in/assam-boosts-ease-of-doing-business-in-tourism-with-single-window-registration-auto-renewals)

In detail: [Cabinet decisions](https://cm.assam.gov.in/cabinet-decisions-details?articleId=924301)

**Odisha** notifies **changes to the English spellings of 64 administrative locations across the state in a move aimed at bringing official records in line with Odia phonetics**. The decision was taken by Odisha's Revenue and Disaster Management Department.

**Source:** [The Wire](https://m.thewire.in/article/government/kataka-for-cuttack-raurkela-for-rourkela-and-more-odisha-changes-spellings-of-64-places/amp)

**In detail:** [Notification](https://revenue.odisha.gov.in/sites/default/files/2026-06/2676-Gazette%20Notification.pdf)

**Water and Sanitation**

**Andhra Pradesh** approves a **Policy on Reuse of Treated Used Water, 2026.** The policy establishes a framework for the collection, treatment, allocation, pricing, and reuse of treated wastewater across all Urban Local Bodies (ULBs).

**Source:** [Deccan Chronicle](https://www.deccanchronicle.com/southern-states/andhra-pradesh/andhra-pradesh-clears-policy-on-treated-water-reuse-1966472)
