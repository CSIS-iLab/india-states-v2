<br/>#Delhi has introduced its **EV Policy 2.0**, shifting towards a **scrappage-linked model** to phase out older, polluting vehicles and accelerate clean mobility.

The policy adopts a scrappage-linked approach, incentivizing the replacement of older, polluting vehicles and offers incentives of up to$1,055 (INR 1 lakh) for private electric cars.

#Uttar Pradesh has approved the Uttar Pradesh Private Business Park Scheme, 2025_,_ strengthening its industrial development strategy.

The scheme allows private developers to build business parks on government land leased for **45 years**, and sub-lease plots to MSMEs and other firms, with developers sharing a portion of lease revenues with the state.
