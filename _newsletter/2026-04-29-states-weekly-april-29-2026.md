**States Weekly: April 29, 2026**

**Big News**: **Uttar Pradesh** releases draft **Unified Building Regulations for Industrial Development Authorities; Maharashtra approves** restructuring of its state-owned distribution company, **Mahavitran**; **Maharashtra** approves the **Maharashtra Compressed Biogas (CBG) Policy, 2026**; **Andhra Pradesh** reduces **Vacant Land Tax; Madhya Pradesh** amends **The Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Aсt, 2015**; and more

**Health** 

**Haryana** notifies **‘Haryana Pneumoconiosis Rehabilitation Policy’** for the rehabilitation of workers affected by pneumoconiosis.

**Source**: [Tribune](https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/haryana/rs-5-lakh-aid-rs-4000-pension-haryana-notifies-rehab-policy-for-pneumoconiosis-patients/)

**Nagaland** launches a new program to improve grassroots service delivery. The state government will bring brings together the Department of School Education, Department of Health and Family Welfare, District Administrations, and Eleutheros Christian Society under a unified convergence framework to strengthen maternal and child health, nutrition, and early childhood development.

**Source:** [India Today](https://www.indiatodayne.in/nagaland/story/nagaland-signs-convergence-pact-to-boost-maternal-child-health-services-in-eastern-districts-1380782-2026-04-24)

**Climate Change and Energy**

Andhra Pradesh determines **Pooled Cost of Power Purchase**. The common order issued by the Andhra Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (APERC) fixed the rate at INR 5.17 per unit, which will be applicable for the financial year 2025–26 under the state’s Renewable Power Purchase Obligation (RPO) regulations.

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/04/23/aperc-sets-pooled-power-purchase-cost-at-%E2%82%B95-17-unit-for-fy26-directs-discom-adjustments-in-andhra-pradesh/)

**In detail**: [APERC order](https://www.aperc.gov.in/admin/upload/OrderOPNo1012and13of2026.pdf)

**Maharashtra** approves overhaul of its state-owned distribution company, **MAHAVITRAN.**

The state government approved a comprehensive financial and structural overhaul aimed at improving efficiency and reducing losses to support the state’s ambition of becoming a "USD 1 trillion economy"

**Source:** [Mid-day](https://www.mid-day.com/news/india-news/article/maharashtra-governmrnt-clears-mahavitaran-overhaul-with-agri-split-and-ipo-roadmap-23627003)

**In detail:** [MSEDCL Restructuring Plan](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:f2aa6131-541a-4889-b6ae-358ccabfe090)

**Maharashtra** approves the **Maharashtra Compressed Biogas (CBG) Policy, 2026**. A key component of the policy, approved by the state cabinet, is the development of a circular economy model. The policy aims to set up compressed biogas projects in every district through Public-Private Partnership (PPP) and hybrid annuity models, and position CBG as a viable alternative fuel.

**Source:** [Deccan Chronicle](https://www.deccanchronicle.com/nation/maharashtra-cabinet-clears-cbg-policy-2026-to-turn-waste-into-energy-1952192)

**Rajasthan** amends the **Central Electricity Authority (Measures relating to Safety and Electricity Supply) Regulations, 2023**. As per the revised norms, the state government approves self-certification of electrical fitting and installation for connections up to 33 kV and generation units up to 1 MVA. Previously, for electricity connections up to 33 kV and small production units, it was mandatory to obtain inspection and approval from the Electrical Inspector.

**Source:** [Bhaskar English](https://www.bhaskarenglish.in/amp/local/rajasthan/news/rajasthan-electricity-connection-rules-self-certification-33kv-137777967.html)

**In detail:** [Press release](https://energy.rajasthan.gov.in/energy/#/home/press-release/239320)

**Industrial Policy and Business Regulations**  

**Uttar Pradesh** unveils the **Unified Building Regulations for Industrial Development Authorities of Uttar Pradesh, 2026**. The draft regulations released by the state government is aimed at standardizing construction norms across industrial development authorities in the state. The proposed regulations have been opened for public consultation, inviting feedback from stakeholders, industry experts, developers, and citizens.

**Source:** [Tennews](https://tennews.in/uttar-pradesh-releases-draft-unified-building-regulations-2026-seeks-public-feedback/)

**In detail:** [Unified Building Regulations for Industrial Development Authorities of Uttar Pradesh, 2026](https://niveshmitra.up.nic.in/Documents/DraftPolicies/Unified_Building_Regulations_for_Industrial_Development_Authorities.pdf)

**Land Acquisition and Labor Regulations**  

**Andhra Pradesh** approves reduction in **Vacant Land Tax (VLT)**. A 50 per cent reduction in vacant land tax by the state government aims to incentivise building activity and ease financial pressure on property owners and developers across urban areas during the construction period. This measure is expected to improve project viability, encourage timely completion, and enhance land utilization across urban local bodies.

**Source:** [Propnewstime](https://www.propnewstime.com/getdetailsStories/Mjk0NDY=/andhra-pradesh-cuts-vacant-land-tax-by-50--during-construction-to-boost-housing-activity)

**In detail:** [Andhra Pradesh vacant land tax](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:b8be9968-55f4-4d64-81b2-d4e3c50e85e1)

**Infrastructure and Governance**

**Bihar** approves new **Satellite Townships**. The state cabinet under the new chief minister approved a sweeping infrastructure push centred on planned urban expansion and technology-led development. 11 new satellite townships will be developed around key cities and a dedicated research park will be set-up at IIT-Patna.

**Source:** [Swarajyamag](https://swarajyamag.com/news-brief/new-bihar-cabinet-approves-11-satellite-townships-with-land-curbs-and-master-plans-alongside-iit-patna-research-park)

**In detail:** [Cabinet decisions](https://state.bihar.gov.in/cache/1/Smart%20City/Cabinet%20Decisions/d22042026.pdf)

**Madhya Pradesh** amends **The Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Aсt, 2015** to increase land acquisition compensation. The state cabinet increased the compensation for farmers whose land is acquired for development projects. Under the revised policy, compensation for land acquired in rural areas will be increased up to four times the market value.

**Source:** [India Today](https://www.indiatoday.in/india/story/madhya-pradesh-cabinet-approves-fourfold-compensation-for-farmers-land-acquisition-2900729-2026-04-24)

**In detail:** [Cabinet decisions](https://www.mpinfo.org/Home/CabinetDetails?newsid=%C2%B2%C2%B0%C2%B2%C2%B6%C2%B0%C2%B4%C2%B2%C2%B2%C3%8E%C2%B5%C2%B2&fontname=%C3%8D%C3%A1%C3%AE%C3%A7%C3%A1%C3%AC&LocID=%C2%B3%C2%B2&pubdate=04/22/2026)

**Goa** approves **Goa Motor Vehicle Tax Amendment Ordinance 2026.** The state cabinet approval paves the way for a revised taxation system for construction equipment vehicles. Under the new framework, a one-time tax will be levied at the time of registration, replacing the existing system of recurring taxes.

**Source:** [Goemkarponn](https://goemkarponn.com/goa-cabinet-clears-one-time-tax-for-construction-vehicles-approves-library-policy-extends-debt-relief-scheme-till-2027/)

**Odisha** approves **City Gas Distribution Policy** to promote clean energy infrastructure through city gas distribution, enhancing the state’s energy framework.

**Source:** [Prameyanews](https://www.prameyanews.com/odisha-cabinet-approves-8-key-proposals-including-city-gas-distribution-policy)

**Odisha** approves **Odisha State Data Policy 2.0** to revise data policy to streamline the management and use of data in the state.

**Source:** [Prameyanews](https://www.prameyanews.com/odisha-cabinet-approves-8-key-proposals-including-city-gas-distribution-policy)

**Odisha** approves creation of **‘Odisha State Allied and Healthcare Council Rules, 2026’.** The decision in line with the National Commission for Allied and Healthcare Professions Act, 2021.

**Source:** [Prameyanews](https://www.prameyanews.com/odisha-cabinet-approves-8-key-proposals-including-city-gas-distribution-policy)
