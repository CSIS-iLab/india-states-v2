**States Weekly Newsletter**

**March 4, 2026**

**Big News: Bihar** passes the **Bihar Micro Finance Institutions (Regulation of Money Lending and Prevention of Coercive Action) Bill, 2026; Rajasthan** approves the **Rajasthan Industrial Park Incentive Policy 2026; Andhra Pradesh** passes the **Andhra Pradesh Assigned Lands (Prohibition of Transfers) (Amendment) Bill, 2026; Andhra Pradesh** approves the **Andhra Pradesh Sick and Stressed MSME Revival Policy (4.0) 2026-31; The Union government** approves the proposal to **change the name of Kerala to Keralam;** and more.

**Health** 

**Delhi** announces an expansion in the eligibility criteria for the **‘Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana’ (Prime Minister’s Public Health Scheme)**. Widows with pensions and persons with disabilities receiving government pensions are now eligible for treatment under the program.

**Source:** [India.com](https://www.india.com/news/india/cm-rekha-gupta-cabinet-decision-boosts-ayushman-bharat-in-delhi-extends-free-health-cover-to-vulnerable-pension-holders-8321005/)

**Karnataka** announces a **State Action Plan for Rabies Elimination (SAPRE) and a State Action Plan for Prevention and Control of Snakebite Envenoming (SAPSE)**. Under SAPRE, free Anti-Rabies Vaccine (ARV) and Rabies Immunoglobulin (RIG) will be provided at all primary health centers, community health centers, and hospitals.

**Source:** [Deccan Herald](https://www.deccanherald.com/india/karnataka/karnataka-announces-action-plans-for-victims-of-dogs-and-snake-bites-3914743)

**Karnataka** launches the **'Kusuma Sanjeevini' (Life-Giving Flower) Program**. Under the program, patients suffering from hemophilia will be provided treatment at no cost. ​

**Source:** [NDTV](https://www.ndtv.com/health/k-taka-launches-free-medicine-programme-for-haemophilia-patients-11135050)

**Uttarakhand** declares **Leprosy a Notifiable Disease.** Under the rules, all diagnosed cases of Leprosy must be reported to District Leprosy Officers.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/dehradun/uttarakhand-declares-leprosy-notifiable-disease/articleshow/128759414.cms)

**Climate Change and Energy**

**Telangana** issues the **(TGERC)** **Framework for Resource Adequacy Regulation,** **2026.** The regulations issued by the Telangana Electricity Regulatory Commission (TGERC) aim to strengthen power planning and ensure a reliable electricity supply. It applies to all key stakeholders, including generating companies, transmission utilities, the State Load Dispatch Center (SLDC), and distribution companies (DISCOMs).

**Source:** [Solar Quarter](https://solarquarter.com/2026/02/26/tgerc-notifies-resource-adequacy-regulation-2026-to-strengthen-telanganas-power-planning/)

**In detail:** [Telangana Electricity Regulatory Commission (Framework for Resource Adequacy) Regulation, 2026](https://www.tgerc.telangana.gov.in/file_upload/uploads/Regulations/Final/tserc/2026/TGERC%20RA%20Regulations%202026.pdf)

**Tripura** issues a **(TERC)** **Third Amendment to the Tripura Electricity Regulatory Commission (Electricity Supply Code) Regulations, 2011**. The amended regulations issued by the Tripura Electricity Regulatory Commission (TERC) aim to incorporate the Union government’s “Ease of Living” directives for consumers.

**Source:** [Mercom](https://www.mercomindia.com/tripura-issues-third-amendment-to-2011-electricity-supply-regulations/)

**In detail:** [TERC (Electricity Supply Code) Regulations, 2011 (Third Amendment), 2025](https://terc.tripura.gov.in/sites/default/files/documents/Tripura%20Electricity%20Regulatory%20Commission%20%28Electricity%20Supply%20Code%29%20Regulations%2C%202011%20%28Third%20Amendment%29%2C%202025.pdf)

**Finance**

**Bihar** passes the **Bihar Micro Finance Institutions (Regulation of Money Lending and Prevention of Coercive Action) Bill, 2026.** The bill introduces new regulations for the regional micro-lending sector. The legislation specifically targets individuals, partnership firms, companies, societies, and trusts that are actively involved in providing micro-loans or small loans within the state.

**Source:** [NDTV](https://www.ndtvprofit.com/markets/bihar-tightens-microfinance-rules-citi-ms-see-no-immediate-hit-to-listed-mfis-11143863)

**In detail:** [The BMFI (Reg. of Money Lending & Prevention of Coercive Actions) Bill, 2026](https://egazette.bihar.gov.in/GazettePublished/238_2_2026.pdf#page=1)

**Industrial Policy and Business Regulations**  

**Rajasthan** approves the **Rajasthan Industrial Park Incentive Policy 2026.** The policy aims to integrate private developers to expand industrial infrastructure, promote private investment, and generate large-scale employment across the state.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/jaipur/govt-allows-bigger-role-for-pvt-firms-in-industrial-park-devpt/articleshow/128824679.cms)

**Punjab** extends the **One-Time Settlement (OTS) Scheme until June 30, 2026**. The scheme allows industry and taxpayers to settle their dues from 2025 without incurring additional penalties.

**Source:** [Babushahi](https://www.babushahi.com/full-news.php?id=217570)

**Punjab** approves a new **Excise Policy, 2026–27.** The new policy permits malt manufacturing (single malt) units in Punjab, enabling both distilling and bottling within the state.

**Source:** [Babushahi](https://www.babushahi.com/full-news.php?id=217570)

**In detail:** [Excise Policy for FY 2026-27](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:97fcabfb-3b41-4e5b-846e-1741322be3e3)

**Land Acquisition and Labor Regulations**  

Nothing critical to report this week.

**Infrastructure and Governance**

**Andhra Pradesh** passes the **Andhra Pradesh Assigned Lands (Prohibition of Transfers) (Amendment) Bill,** **2026\.** The bill enables the government to consider acquiring assigned lands on a long-term lease basis for setting up renewable energy projects under the A.P. Integrated Clean Energy Policy 2024.

**Source:** [Deccan Chronicle](https://www.deccanchronicle.com/southern-states/andhra-pradesh/assigned-lands-swarna-rebranding-bills-get-ap-assembly-nod-1940164)

**Andhra Pradesh** establishes a new **Science, Technology and Innovation Department.** The aim of the department is to focus on innovation to drive economic growth.

**Source:** [New Indian Express](https://www.newindianexpress.com/states/andhra-pradesh/2026/Feb/25/andhra-pradesh-cabinet-gives-nod-to-set-up-new-science-technology-and-innovation-department)

**Andhra Pradesh** approves the **Andhra Pradesh Sick and Stressed Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises** (**MSME) Revival Policy (4.0) 2026-31.** The policy aims to revitalize distressed MSME units and promote entrepreneurship through subsidies on loan interest, power costs, and technology upgrades.

**Source:** [New Indian Express](https://www.newindianexpress.com/states/andhra-pradesh/2026/Feb/25/andhra-pradesh-cabinet-gives-nod-to-set-up-new-science-technology-and-innovation-department)

**Andhra Pradesh** releases draft **Andhra Pradesh Coaching Institutions (Regulation and Control) Rules, 2026.** The rules propose changes to how private coaching centers function in the state, including restrictions on operating times and mandatory mental health screenings.

**Source:** [Indian Express](https://indianexpress.com/article/education/andhra-pradesh-drafts-coaching-institute-regulations-fee-transparency-engineering-cuet-jee-main-nta-10554005/)

**In detail:** [Draft Andhra Pradesh Coaching Institutions (Regulation & Control) Rules, 2026](https://apcce.gov.in/Draft_Coaching_Center_Guidelines_in_AP.pdf)

**Bihar** passes the **Bihar Jan Vishwas (Provision Amendment) Bill, 2026.** The bill aims to create a business-friendly environment by amending stringent laws and provisions.

**Source:** [Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/patna/bihar-assembly-clears-six-reform-bills-to-woo-investors-curb-mfi-harassment-private-college-fees-overhauled/amp_articleshow/128823281.cms)

**In detail:** [Bihar Jan Vishwas (Provision Amendment) Bill, 2026](https://egazette.bihar.gov.in/GazettePublished/241_2_2026.pdf#page=1) (in Hindi)

**Telangana** approves an **accident insurance scheme and a cashless health insurance scheme for all government employees and pensioners in the state**. Treatment will be completely cashless in all government hospitals and 652 empaneled private hospitals. This will cover 1,998 medical procedures.

**Source:** [News Minute](https://www.thenewsminute.com/telangana/telangana-cabinet-approves-insurance-health-schemes-for-employees-pensioners)

**Assam** launches the **Chief Minister’s ‘Jibon Prerana’ (Life Inspiration) Scheme.** The scheme provides monthly financial assistance of $27.43 (INR 2,500) for 12 months to 48,319 fresh graduates to support their career progression and transition into employment or higher studies.

**Source:** [NE Today](https://northeasttoday.in/northeast/assam/assam-cm-sarma-launches-financial-aid-schemes-and-industry-4-0-centres-for-youth-empowerment/)

**Assam** launches the **Chief Minister’s ‘Jibon Anuprerana’ (Life Encouragement) Scheme.** The scheme will provide a one-time financial stipend ranging from $274.34 to $440 (INR 25,000 to 40,000) to 2,112 research scholars with the aim of encouraging research, innovation, and academic advancement.

**Source:** [NE Today](https://northeasttoday.in/northeast/assam/assam-cm-sarma-launches-financial-aid-schemes-and-industry-4-0-centres-for-youth-empowerment/)

**Kerala** launches the **Assured Pension Scheme (APS) for its government employees with effect from April 1, 2026.** With the new scheme, state government employees, existing and new, can join either the National Pension System (NPS) or the Assured Pension Scheme.

**Source:** [Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/wealth/save/assured-pension-scheme-for-government-employees-announced-by-kerala-government-50-pension-key-features-and-eligibility-explained/articleshow/128910105.cms)

**In detail:** [Kerala Finance Department – Notification](https://acrobat.adobe.com/id/urn:aaid:sc:VA6C2:3f2d2dcd-ea0c-49ab-bb66-4cb03baaa98f)

**Meghalaya** passes the **Meghalaya Prison and Correctional Services Bill, 2026.** It provides a framework to ensure that transgender prisoners are provided adequate protections and given access to essential services tailored to their needs.

**Source:** [Pratidin](https://www.pratidintime.com/north-east/meghalaya/meghalaya-passes-progressive-prison-bill-for-transgender-inmates-11164183)

**Mizoram** amends the **Mizo Marriage, Divorce and Inheritance of Property Act, 2014.** The legislation includes a formal ban on polygamy and bigamy.

**Source:** [India Today](https://www.indiatodayne.in/mizoram/story/mizoram-legislative-assembly-passes-bill-to-amend-mizo-marriage-laws-bans-polygamy-1351229-2026-02-24)

**In detail:** [Mizo Marriage and Inheritance of Property (Amendment) Act, 2026](https://prsindia.org/files/bills_acts/bills_states/mizoram/2026/Bill33of2026MZ.pdf)

**Center – State Relations**   

**The Union government** approves the proposal to **change the name of Kerala to Keralam.** The President of India will now refer a Bill, namely the Kerala (Alteration of Name) Bill, 2026 to the Legislative Assembly of Kerala for its review before it is presented to the Indian parliament.

**Source:** [News on AIR](https://www.newsonair.gov.in/union-cabinet-approves-renaming-state-of-kerala-to-keralam-2/)

**In detail:** [Press release](https://pib.gov.in/PressReleasePage.aspx?PRID=2232123)

**Himachal Pradesh** launches a **Vintage Vehicle Policy**. Vehicles registered before 1975 will be categorized as vintage, exempting them from age-related scrapping policies.

**Source:** [Devdiscourse](https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/business/3817552-himachal-pradesh-embraces-vintage-vehicles-with-new-policy)
