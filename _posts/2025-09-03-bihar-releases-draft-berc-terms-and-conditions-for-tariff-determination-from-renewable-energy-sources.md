Bihar releases draft BERC (Terms and Conditions for Tariff Determination from Renewable Energy Sources) Regulations, 2025. The regulations, issued by the Bihar Electricity Regulatory Commission (BERC) are designed to govern tariﬀ determination for all renewable energy projects due for commissioning during the financial years 2025-26 to 2027-28.
