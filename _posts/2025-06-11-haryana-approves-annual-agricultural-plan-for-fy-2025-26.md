Haryana approves Annual Agricultural Plan for FY 2025-26. The plan approved by Haryana State Level Sanctioning Committee under the “**Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (PM-RKVY)” proposes innovative initiatives such as on-farm water management, natural farming, mechanization, and scientific innovation.
