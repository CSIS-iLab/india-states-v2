Bihar approves the Bihar Renewable Energy Policy 2025. The new policy approved by the state government will be applicable for the period of five years and sets a target of deploying 23.968 GW of Renewable Energy and 6.1 GWh of Energy Storage by FY 2029-30. The new policy oﬀers a wide range of incentives for projects commissioned during this period such as full exemption from Electricity duty, State GST, Transmission and Wheeling charges and full reimbursement of Stamp duty, and Land conversion charges
