Uttarakhand approves several new policies. The state introduces the Witness Protection Scheme, 2025 to protect witnesses from intimidation and the Uttarakhand Crime Victim Assistance (Amendment) Scheme, 2025, which provides compensation to victims of heinous crimes. The state also introduces skill development and training programs to expand job opportunities in both government and private sectors, including coaching classes in foreign languages and artificial intelligence.
