Kerala issues draft Kerala State Electricity Regulatory Commission (Renewable Energy and Related Matters) Regulations, 2025. The draft regulations issued by Kerala State Electricity Regulatory Commission (KSERC) apply to matters such as determination of tariﬀ of energy from Renewable Energy systems including Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) and Pumped Storage Plants, Renewable Energy Metering and Billing etc.
