Assam releases the Assam Integrated Clean Energy Policy, 2025 aimed at promoting renewable energy and grid integration in the state. The policy aims to achieve an additional 11.7 GW of renewable energy projects in the state by 2029-30. The policy aims to provide a framework for pumped storage power, green hydrogen, and renewable energy.
