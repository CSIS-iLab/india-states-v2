Uttar Pradesh approves Tariff for Wind-Solar hybrid power project. The approval to the levelized tariﬀ and Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) for a 300 MW grid‑connected wind-solar hybrid power project was accorded by the Uttar Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (UPERC). The hybrid project will supply power to Noida Power Company Limited (NPCL).
