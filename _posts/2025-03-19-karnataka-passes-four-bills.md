Karnataka passes four bills – the Karnataka Micro Loan and Small Loan (Prevention of Coercive Actions) Bill 2025, the Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Bill 2025, the Karnataka Prohibition of Charging Exorbitant Interest (Amendment) Bill 2025 and the Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Bill 2025 to address the financial exploitation of borrowers and curb the practice of usury by moneylenders.
