Karnataka passes the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2025. The amendment introduces provisions for a one-time lifetime tax system, replacing the earlier model of recurring annual taxes, and significantly alters how private and commercial vehicles—including electric vehicles —are taxed across the state. EVs priced above $30,000—previously exempt from road tax—will now be taxed at a 10 percent lifetime rate.
