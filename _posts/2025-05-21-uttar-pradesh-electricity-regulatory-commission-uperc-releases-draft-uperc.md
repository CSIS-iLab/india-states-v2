title: Uttar Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (UPERC) releases draft
  UPERC (Captive and Renewable Energy Generating Plants) Regulations, 2024 to
  solicit public comments. The draft regulation will apply to all Captive
  Generating Plants (Non-RE) having an installed capacity of 1 MW or above, and
  all Captive Generating Plants (RE) and Renewable Energy Generating Plants
  having an installed capacity of 100 kW and above. The Commission will hold a
  public hearing on this matter on June 3, 2025, and all stakeholders must
  submit their written comments by May 30, 2025.
