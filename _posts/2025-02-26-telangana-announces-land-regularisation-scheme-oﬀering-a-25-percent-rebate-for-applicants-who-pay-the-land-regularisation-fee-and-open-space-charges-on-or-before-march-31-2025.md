Goa notifies the Goa Legislative Diploma No. 2070 (Amendment) Act, 2025, which introduces new provisions allowing the Administrator of Comunidades (land associations in Goa) to reclaim land that has been used for purposes other than those for which it was originally granted. The reversion of such land to the Comunidade will require the approval of the government.
