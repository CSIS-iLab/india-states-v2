Manipur Chief Minister N Biren Singh resigns from his post amid ongoing violence in the state. The BJP is yet to announce a new chief minister for the state. BJP led NDA currently holds 52 out of 60 seats in the state’s legislative assembly, and the BJP is the single largest party with 37 seats.
