Delhi revokes pollution curbs imposed under the Graded Response Action Plan (GRAP) Stage III. The Commission for Air Quality Management (CAQM) states that air quality has improved in the National Capital Region (NCR) of Delhi, and curbs can now be rolled back. The ban on non-essential diesel-run vehicles has been withdrawn, and the transport of construction materials, including cement, sand and fly ash, is now permitted. Construction activities can also resume.
