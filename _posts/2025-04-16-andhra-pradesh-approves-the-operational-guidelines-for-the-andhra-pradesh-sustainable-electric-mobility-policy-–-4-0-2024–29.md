Andhra Pradesh approves the operational guidelines for the Andhra Pradesh Sustainable Electric Mobility Policy – 4.0 (2024–29). The policy aims to position the state as a global hub for high-value electric vehicle manufacturing through strategic demand- and supply-side initiatives. It sets a long-term goal of achieving carbon neutrality in the transport sector by 2047.
