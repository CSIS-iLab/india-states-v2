Karnataka launches the Karnataka Clean Mobility Policy, 2025-30. The policy aims to generate investments of $5.75 billion across the clean mobility value chain and create 100,000 new jobs in the sector. Key highlights include an incentive package, with the government oﬀering subsidies of up to 25 percent on total invested capex across the state.
