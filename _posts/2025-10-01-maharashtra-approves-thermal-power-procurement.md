Maharashtra approves Thermal Power Procurement. The order, issued by Maharasthra Electricity Regulatory Commission (MERC), is based on petitions filed by the Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) regarding the procurement of medium-term power. The petitions highlighted the need to ensure reliable supply for the state in the coming years, while also showing a clear long-term direction toward renewable energy, especially solar. MSEDCL sought approval for bidding documents and the adoption of tariﬀs discovered through competitive bidding for the procurement of 993 megawatts of thermal power for a period of five years, beginning November 1, 2025, and ending October 31, 2030.
