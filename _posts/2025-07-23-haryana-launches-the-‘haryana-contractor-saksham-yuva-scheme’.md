Haryana launches the ‘Haryana Contractor Saksham Yuva Scheme’. The scheme launched by the state government is aimed at providing skill training and employment opportunities to ITI, diploma and engineering graduates in the state. Under the scheme, the eligible candidates will undergo 90-day training at Shri Vishwakarma Skill University, Palwal. On successful completion, they will be empanelled as contractors on Haryana Engineering Works Portal (HEWP).
