Delhi signs a memorandum of understanding with the Union Ministry of Health and Family Welfare to implement the “Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana”(AB-PMJAY) in the city, becoming the 35th state/Union Territory to implement the health insurance scheme. West Bengal is now the only state which has not implemented the scheme.
