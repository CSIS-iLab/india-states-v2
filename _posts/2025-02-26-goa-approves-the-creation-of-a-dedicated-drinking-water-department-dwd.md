Goa approves the creation of a dedicated Drinking Water Department (DWD). This new department will be responsible for ensuring a reliable water supply to the residents of Goa. The state’s Public Works Department will henceforth focus solely on maintaining roads and buildings, while the DWD will handle water supply.
