Assam approves ‘Shradhanjali’ scheme. This new scheme applicable primarily for youth engaged in low-paying jobs outside the state enables the state government to facilitate an easy, lawful and coordinated transportation of dead bodies who are of the domicile of the state of Assam from other states to Assam, among others.
