Uttar Pradesh approves the Urban Green Policy. The proposed policy of the state government aims to develop a ‘Green City Monitoring System’ to evaluate and award ‘Green Star’ ratings to cities based on their environmental eﬀorts and green infrastructure. The policy will be funded through various central and state government programmes such as AMRUT 2.0, National Clean Air Programme, and grants from the Central and State Finance Commissions
