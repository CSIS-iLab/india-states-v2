Rajasthan approves the draft Medical Value Travel Policy, 2025. The policy also known as Heal in Rajasthan Policy is aimed at developing the state as a destination for accessible and aﬀordable medical services. The state government will establish a Medical Value Travel (MVT) cell to certify facilitators and service providers.
