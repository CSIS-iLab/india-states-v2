Chhattisgarh presents the State Budget, 2025-26 with an outlay of $18.92 billion. The budget includes cuts on the value added taxes on petrol from April 1 which will lower the prices of the fuel by $0.0114 per litre. Additionally, it proposes the creation of a fund for future liabilities of pension of government employees and earmarking $1.146 billion for boosting farm prosperity, he said
