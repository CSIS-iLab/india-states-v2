Bihar approves drinking water projects in Darbhanga and Aurangabad under the AMRUT 2.0 scheme. In Aurangabad, water distribution network will be developed to provide new water connections. Darbhanga will receive a more comprehensive infrastructure overhaul with household water connections, distribution network, tube wells etc.
