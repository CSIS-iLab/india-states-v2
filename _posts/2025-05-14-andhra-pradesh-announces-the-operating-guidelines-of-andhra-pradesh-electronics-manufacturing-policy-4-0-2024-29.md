Andhra Pradesh announces the Operating Guidelines of Andhra Pradesh Electronics Manufacturing Policy (4.0), 2024-29, aiming to facilitate the production of electronic goods worth $49.16 billion, attract investments amounting to $10 billion, and generate employment for 500,000 people. As per the guidelines, Sub- large projects are eligible for a capital investment subsidy of 20 percent over 5 years, large projects receive 25 percent over 7 years; and mega projects can claim 30 percent over 10 years. Additionally, the state is oﬀering recruitment assistance of $48 and $72 for male and female employees respectively.
