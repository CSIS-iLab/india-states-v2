Delhi amends the Delhi Solar Energy Policy 2023. The amendment notified by the state government increases the state capital subsidy for residential rooftop solar systems to INR 10,000 (~$116.28) per kW, capped at INR 30,000 (~$348.84) for systems with a capacity of up to 3 kW. The amendment also introduces a new capital subsidy for group housing societies (GHS) and resident welfare associations (RWAs).
