Maharashtra introduces the Sand Mining Policy, 2025 to curb illegal mining and improve access to sand for public use. Under the policy, 10 percent of sand from each mining block will be allocated free of cost for government housing schemes, villagers, and farmers. Beneficiaries building homes under government schemes, as well as rural residents and farmers, will receive 5 brass of sand at no charge to support village development and farm well construction.
