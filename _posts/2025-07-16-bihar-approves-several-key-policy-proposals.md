Bihar approves several key policy proposals. In a series of policy decisions aimed towards inclusivity and rural support, the state government has approved the **Chief Minister Divyangjan Empowerment Scheme ‘Sambal’**, for providing financial assistance to male Divyangjan candidates from backward classes, economically weaker sections, and general categories clearing civil services prelims. To counter the impact of irregular monsoon or drought, the government released **Diesel Subsidy for FY 2025-26. The cabinet also approved the **Corporate Social Responsibility Policy 2025 and formation of Bihar State Corporate Social Responsibility Society to monitor and eﬀectively implement CSR funds.
