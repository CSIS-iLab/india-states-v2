Rajasthan approves the Rajasthan City Gas Distribution (CGD) Policy, 2025. The policy seeks to strengthen the gas-based energy framework within the state. To streamline the regulatory process, the policy introduces simplified and time-bound procedures for City Gas Distribution (CGD) companies.
