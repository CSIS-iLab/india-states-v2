Karnataka approves amendments to the Karnataka Exorbitant Rate of Interest Act and the Pawnbrokers Regulatory Act aimed at strengthening penalties against cooperative societies and pawnbrokers charging exorbitant interest rates. The amendments come after the state government’s microfinance regulatory ordinance, which include increasing imprisonment terms and penalties for violations.
