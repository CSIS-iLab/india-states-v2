Ministry of Power approves subsidy for 30 GWh of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS). The Viability Gap Funding (VGF) scheme for development of BESS will be supported through Power System Development Fund (PSDF). The ministry has decided to provide the VGF to 15 states and National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC).
