Bihar issues the draft BERC (Deviation Settlement Mechanism and Related Matters) Regulations, 2025. Issued by the Bihar Electricity Regulatory Commission (BERC), the regulations apply to all intra-state and grid-connected entities in Bihar, including distribution licensees and deemed distribution licensees. They also apply to open access consumers (both partial and full) connected to the intra-state transmission system (InSTS). The renewable energy generation stations connected to these InSTSs must have at least 5 MW of exportable capacity.
