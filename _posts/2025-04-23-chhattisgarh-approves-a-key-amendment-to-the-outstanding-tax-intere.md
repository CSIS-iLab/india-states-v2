Chhattisgarh approves a key amendment to the **Outstanding Tax, Interest, and Penalty Settlement Ordinance, 2025, waiving value added tax (VAT) liabilities up to ₹25,000 in cases pending for over 10 years. The move is expected to benefit over 40,000 small traders and reduce more than 62,000 litigation cases, creating a more supportive environment for MSMEs.
