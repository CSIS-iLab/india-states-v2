Rajasthan releases the Rajasthan State Budget 2025-26. Terming it as the state’s first “Green Budget,” the government laid special emphasis on climate change adaptation, forests, environment, and biodiversity. The government announced 150 units of free electricity per month to households installing solar plants and also announced community solar plants for low-income families.
