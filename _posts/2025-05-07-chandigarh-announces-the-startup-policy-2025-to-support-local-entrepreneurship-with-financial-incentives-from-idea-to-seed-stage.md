Chandigarh announces the Startup Policy, 2025 to support local entrepreneurship with financial incentives from idea to seed stage. The policy provides benefits for startups up to 10 years old or with annual turnover below $12 million, and outlines grievance mechanisms for cases of fraud or misrepresentation.
