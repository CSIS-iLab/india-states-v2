Himachal Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission issues an Order setting levelized tariﬀs for small solar photovoltaic (PV) projects (up to 5 MW) for FY 2025–26.  The final capital cost was revised upwards to $403,835–$424,035 per MW depending on project size, with an added $9,036 per MW for urban and industrial areas. Tariﬀs are fixed at $0.0416/kWh (up to 1 MW), $0.0407/kWh (1–3 MW), and $0.0400/kWh (3–5 MW), with slightly higher rates for urban areas. A $0.0006 per unit royalty is also mandated for projects above 1 MW.
