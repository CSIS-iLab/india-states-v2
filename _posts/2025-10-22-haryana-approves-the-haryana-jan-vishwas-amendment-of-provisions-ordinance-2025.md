Haryana approves the Haryana Jan Vishwas (Amendment of Provisions) Ordinance, 2025. The ordinance aims to reduce compliance burdens, decriminalize minor oﬀenses, and replace criminal penalties with civil and administrative actions, aligning with the Union government’s Jan Vishwas initiative.
