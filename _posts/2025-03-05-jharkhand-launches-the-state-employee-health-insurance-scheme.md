Jharkhand launches the State Employee Health Insurance Scheme. Under the scheme, state government employees will be able to avail cashless treatment up to $5,700 (₹5 lakh), and up to $11,400 (₹10 lakh) in case of serious diseases, in empanelled hospitals nationwide.
