Chhattisgarh notifies “Terms and Conditions for Determination of Tariff for Renewable Energy Sources Regulations, 2025. The regulation applies to renewable energy projects that will begin commercial operations between April 1, 2025, and March 31, 2030, and the Chhattisgarh State Electricity Regulatory Commission (CSERC) will determine both generic and project-specific tariﬀs.
