Assam approves the Assam Aerospace and Defence Manufacturing Policy, 2025 with the aim to promote indigenous defense manufacturing and development in the state. The policy oﬀers financial incentives including tax exemptions for firms, and also focuses on skill development by creating technical institutes and training centers.
