Tamil Nadu Online Gaming Authority (TNOGA) introduces the Tamil Nadu Online Gaming Authority (Real Money Games) Regulations, 2025 to oversee online real money gaming in the state, tightening access and imposing safeguards to protect users including minors. The new rules prohibit minors from participating in real money gaming and make know-your-customer verification mandatory for account creation. These measures aim to enhance security and prevent unauthorised access.
