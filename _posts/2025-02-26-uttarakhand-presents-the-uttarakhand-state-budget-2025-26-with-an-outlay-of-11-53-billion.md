Uttarakhand presents the Uttarakhand State Budget 2025-26 with an outlay of $11.53 billion. The budget focuses on innovation, agriculture, connectivity and infrastructure development. The total budget size has been increased by 13 percent compared to the budgetary allocation of $10.26 billion for the current fiscal.
