Karnataka passes the Karnataka Platform-based Gig Workers (Social Security and Welfare) Bill, 2025. It allows the state to levy a welfare fee of 1 to 5 percent on aggregators to benefit gig workers by providing income security. The bill also establishes a welfare board to frame the rules and determine the welfare fee.
