Delhi amends Supply Code and Performance Standards Regulations, 2017. The regulations titled Delhi Electricity Regulatory Commission (Supply Code and Performance Standards) (Seventh Amendment) Regulations, 2025 divides the infrastructure-related works undertaken by the Distribution Licensee on behalf of Departments of Govt. of NCT of Delhi into three categories to provide a structured approach and bring clarity and accountability.
