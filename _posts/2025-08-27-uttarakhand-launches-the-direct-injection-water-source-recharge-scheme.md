Uttarakhand launches the Direct Injection Water Source Recharge Scheme**. The scheme aims to increase the groundwater level by injecting treated rainwater into inactive hand pumps. The state hopes to revive inactive hand pumps, starting in Gairsain and Chaukhutia areas on a trial basis.
