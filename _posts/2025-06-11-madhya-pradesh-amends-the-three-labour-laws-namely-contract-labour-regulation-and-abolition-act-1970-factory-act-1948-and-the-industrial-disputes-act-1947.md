Madhya Pradesh amends the three labour laws namely Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, Factory Act, 1948 and the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947. The amendments aim to simplify labour laws and reduce compliance burden on small and medium scale institutions and industries. The amendment to Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, 1970, and Factory Act, 1948 raises limit on workers currently prescribed.
