Maharashtra announces phased reductions in Power Tariff. The tariﬀ order of Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) for the control period FY 26 to FY 30 approved by the Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commission (MERC) provides relief to the consumers in 0-100 units consumption category. The initial relief will be of 9 pe cent in FY 26 going up to 26 per cent by FY 30. The tariﬀ for consumers in the consumption category above 100 units will increase by 0.5 to 3 per cent in FY 26 and FY 27, and then gradually decrease in FY 28, FY 29 and FY 30.
