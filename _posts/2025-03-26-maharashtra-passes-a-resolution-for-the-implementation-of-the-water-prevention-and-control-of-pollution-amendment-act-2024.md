Maharashtra passes a resolution for the implementation of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Amendment Act, 2024**, introduced by the central government in February 2024. The Act has provisions for penal action instead of imprisonment for violation of petty oﬀences as well as decriminalising some of them.
