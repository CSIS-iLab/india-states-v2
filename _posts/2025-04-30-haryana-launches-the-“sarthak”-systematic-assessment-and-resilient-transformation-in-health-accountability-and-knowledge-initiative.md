Haryana launches the “SARTHAK” (Systematic Assessment and Resilient Transformation in Health Accountability and Knowledge) initiative. The initiative aims to create a structured assessment and monitoring framework, designed to ensure accountability, improve quality and enhance service delivery across healthcare facilities in the state. Under the initiative, dedicated teams from the State National Health Mission will visit district health facilities for evaluation and also assess staﬀ performance.
