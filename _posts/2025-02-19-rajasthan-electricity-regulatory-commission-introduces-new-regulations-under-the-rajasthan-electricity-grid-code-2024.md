Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission introduces new regulations under the Rajasthan Electricity Grid Code, 2024, aimed at ensuring the efficient operation, management, and safety of the state’s power grid. These regulations focus on defining technical and operational standards for electricity transmission and distribution across the state.
