The Union government passes The Promotion and Regulation of Online Gaming Bill, 2025. The bill seeks to ban all forms of online money games while allowing eSports and online social gaming. The bill regularizes existing legislation at the national and state-level that bans gambling and betting, and extends it to online platforms.
