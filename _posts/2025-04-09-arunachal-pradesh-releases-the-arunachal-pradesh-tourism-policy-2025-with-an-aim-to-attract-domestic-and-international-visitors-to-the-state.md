Arunachal Pradesh releases the Arunachal Pradesh Tourism Policy, 2025 with an aim to attract domestic and international visitors to the state. The policy emphasizes on the development of experiential tourism farm tourism, wine tourism, wellness and spiritual tourism, adventure tourism, film tourism, ecotourism, and tribal tourism.
