Tripura launches Rooftop Solar Program. The program aims to scale up renewable energy generation by utilizing the rooftops of government buildings across the state. TREDA (Tripura Renewable Energy Development Agency) plans to install solar panels on 2,000 government facilities, with the goal of generating around 70 MW of clean electricity.
