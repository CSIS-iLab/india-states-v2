Madhya Pradesh introduces the Madhya PradeshExcise Policy, 2025. Eﬀective from April 1, 2025, the policy will restrict the sale of liquor in 19 places, including 17 religious cities. The policy also introduces the concept of a low alcoholic beverage bar, which will be allowed to serve drinks with a maximum of 10 percent alcohol content.
