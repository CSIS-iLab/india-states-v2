The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) launches the City Accelerator Program. This program is a key initiative under the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana (PMSGMBY) and aims to drive faster adoption of rooftop solar across Indian cities. The program is being implemented in collaboration with Shakti Sustainable Energy Foundation (SSEF) and in the first phase will be rolled out in 30 cities across 10 states.
