Chhattisgarh approves ‘Jan Vishwas Bill’. The bill approved by the state assembly amends 163 provisions across 8 acts such as Urban Administration Department laws, Society Registration Act, Chhattisgarh Industrial Relations Act, Chhattisgarh Excise Act to decriminalise minor technical violations by citizens and business owners with an aim to lessen non-essential legal burdens and encouraging voluntary compliance.
