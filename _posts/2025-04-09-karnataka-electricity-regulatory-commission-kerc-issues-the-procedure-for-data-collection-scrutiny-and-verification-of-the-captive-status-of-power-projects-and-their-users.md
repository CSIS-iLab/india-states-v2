Karnataka Electricity Regulatory Commission (KERC) issues the Procedure for Data Collection, Scrutiny and Verification of the Captive Status of Power Projects and their Users. As per the procedure, the status of captive power projects and captive users will be verified annually, and applications for verification will need to be submitted to the respective power distribution companies before May 31, following the financial year for which captive status is claimed.
