Jharkhand issues the JSERC (Determination of tariff for procurement of power from solar PV power project and solar thermal power project) Regulations, 2025. The draft regulations issued by the state regulator, Jharkhand State Electricity Regulatory Commission (JSERC), would apply to grid connected renewable energy projects whose tariﬀ are determined by the regulator. In addition, these regulations would also apply to solar power projects, floating solar projects, solar thermal power projects, solar hybrid energy projects, and solar-plus-storage projects, subject to eligibility criteria specified in the regulation.
