Uttar Pradesh introduces the Footwear, Leather and Non-Leather Development Area Policy, 2025. Under the policy, the state identifies large plots of land and creates dedicated leather parks that are equipped with plug-and-play facilities, waste treatment plants, and modern infrastructure. The land allocation process will take placethrough a single-window system to ensure ease and transparency.
