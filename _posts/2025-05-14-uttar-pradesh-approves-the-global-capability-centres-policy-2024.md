Uttar Pradesh approves the Global Capability Centres Policy, 2024 aimed at encouraging multinational companies to set up Global Capability Centres (GCCs) aiming to attract over 1,000 new GCCs and create more than 500,000 jobs by leveraging the state’s vast talent pool, strategic location, and expanding infrastructure. The policy provides up to $6 million in capital subsidies, 30–50 percent land subsidies, and 100 percent stamp duty waivers based on location.
