West Bengal forms the State-Level Investment Synergy Committee (SLISC) to expedite the implementation of project proposals and boost ease of doing business in the state. Chaired by the chief secretary, SLISC will act as a single-window clearance system for investment proposals, ensuring approvals within a mandated timeframe.
