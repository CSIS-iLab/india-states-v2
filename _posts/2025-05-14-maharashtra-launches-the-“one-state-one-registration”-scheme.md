Maharashtra launches the “One State, One Registration” scheme allowing citizens to register property documents in any sub-registrar office in the state, regardless of where the property is situated. The move is aimed at making the property registration process citizen friendly. It will not aﬀect property prices or tax rates but could make the buying and selling of properties smoother and more secure.
