Gujarat releases the Gujarat Scheme for Antyodaya Families for Augmenting Livelihoods (G-SAFAL). The scheme aimed at fostering economic independence and women's empowerment, targets 50,000 Antyodaya Anna Yojana cardholder families spread across 25 talukas in 10 districts over five years.
