Punjab amends PSERC (Electricity Supply Code, Standard of Performance and Related Matters) (1st Amendment) Regulations, 2026. The amendments, issued by the Punjab State Electricity Regulatory Commission (PSERC), allow for the release of electricity connections in “abandoned colonies” where builders have failed to complete infrastructure.
