Odisha issues Homestay Policy. The policy, issued by the Tourism Department, focuses on the Sambalpur region. As per the policy, if any individual residing in a village surrounding Sambalpur arranges to host tourists in their home, the state will oﬀer a financial incentive of up to 30 percent for the construction of additional rooms.
