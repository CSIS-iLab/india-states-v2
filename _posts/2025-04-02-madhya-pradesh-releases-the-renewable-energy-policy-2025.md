Madhya Pradesh releases the Renewable Energy Policy, 2025, outlining a comprehensive strategy to increase the state’s renewable energy capacity, attract investments, and integrate green power into its overall energy mix. The policy will remain eﬀective for five years and applies to all renewable energy (RE) projects with a minimum capacity of 500 kilowatts (kW). As per the policy, Madhya Pradesh has set a target to meet 50 percent of its annual power demand through renewable energy sources by 2030.
