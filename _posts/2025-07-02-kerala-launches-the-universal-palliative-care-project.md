Kerala launches the Universal Palliative Care project. This intervention is one of the ten key focus areas of Navakeralam Action Plan’s Ardham Mission 2. The state government also established the Kerala Palliative Care Grid — a tech-enabled initiative that aims to unify healthcare eﬀorts across the state and respond to the rising needs of an ageing population.
