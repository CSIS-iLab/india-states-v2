Tamil Nadu clears the Space Industrial Policy, 2025, targeting $1.2 billion in investments and 10,000 high-value jobs. The policy promotes end-to-end participation in the space-tech value chain through industrial clusters or “Space Bays” in southern districts. Startups and MSMEs will benefit from fast-tracked clearances, while all space-tech companies will receive a 50 percent subsidy on clearance charges.
