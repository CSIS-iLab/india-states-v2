Assam releases the Assam PM Surya Ghar Bidding Document. The document lays down guidelines to operationalize the central government’s “PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana” Solar Rooftop Scheme and has designated the Assam Power Distribution Company Limited as the implementing agency for the project in Assam. Assam seeks to promote rooftop solar deployment using utility led aggregation, under which power distribution companies facilitate the installation of solar power systems, by aggregating demand.
