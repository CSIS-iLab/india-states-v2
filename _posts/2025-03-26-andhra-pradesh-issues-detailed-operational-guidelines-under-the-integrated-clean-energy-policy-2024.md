Andhra Pradesh issues detailed Operational Guidelines under the Integrated Clean Energy Policy 2024. The guidelines cover resource allocation, land allotment, grid connectivity, open access, banking facilities, project migration, fees, and incentives. The guidelines aim to streamline approvals, accelerate project implementation, and enhance the state's renewable energy landscape.
