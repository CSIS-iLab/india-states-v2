Kerala renews the Medisep Health Insurance Scheme. The scheme, designed for state employees and pensioners, increases coverage for major surgeries. For example, beneficiaries can now claim up to $20,567 (INR 18 lakhs) for liver transplants. The scheme’s basic insurance coverage increases from the present $3435 (INR 3 lakh) to $5725 (INR 5 lakh).
