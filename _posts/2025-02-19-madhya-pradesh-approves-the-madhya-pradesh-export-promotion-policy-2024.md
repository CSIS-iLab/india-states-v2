Madhya Pradesh approves the Madhya Pradesh Export Promotion Policy, 2024. Under the policy, the government will provide financial assistance to exporters participating in international trade fairs and exhibitions, as well as provide subsidies on transportation and warehousing costs. The policy also includes the creation of a state level export promotion council.
