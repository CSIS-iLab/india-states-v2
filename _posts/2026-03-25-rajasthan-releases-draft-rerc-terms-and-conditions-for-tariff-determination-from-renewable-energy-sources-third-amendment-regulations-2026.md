Rajasthan releases draft RERC (Terms and Conditions for Tariff Determination from Renewable Energy Sources) (Third Amendment) Regulations, 2026. The regulations, issued by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) propose to extend the validity of the current Renewal Energy Tariff Regulations until March 31, 2028, while introducing changes affecting biomass power projects, energy storage systems, payment discipline, and open access provisions.
