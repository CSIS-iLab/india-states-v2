Odisha approves the continuation of the “Nirmal: Strengthening Ancillary Services at Public Health Facilities” scheme for a period of five years, with an estimated budget of $605.7 million. The scheme aims to provide better ambience and quality care to patients at public health facilities across the state. The Nirmal scheme will cover all public health facilities, including sub-centres, primary health centres, community health centres, district headquarters hospitals, medical colleges, and AYUSH colleges.
