**Karnataka** launches the **draft Global Capability Centres (GCC) Policy**, aiming to attract 500 new GCCs, create 350,000 new jobs, and generate economic output of $50 million by 2029. The draft policy provides incentives for skilling, including technical and soft skill enhancement for graduates and diploma holders.
