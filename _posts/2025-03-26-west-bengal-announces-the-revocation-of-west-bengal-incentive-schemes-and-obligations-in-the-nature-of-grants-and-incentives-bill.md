West Bengal announces the Revocation of West Bengal Incentive Schemes and Obligations in the Nature of Grants and Incentives Bill. The bill revokes eight incentive schemes for heavy industries, which were launched during the 1993-2013 period. The state aims to replace these with a modern incentive scheme to encourage setting up of industrial units.
