Gujarat approves the GERC (Procurement of Energy from Renewable Sources) Regulations, 2025. Issued by the Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC), the regulations focus on “Obligated Entities,” which include distribution companies, consumers who get electricity through open access, and those who operate their own power plants. These entities are now required to meet specific yearly targets for procuring or generating renewable energy.
