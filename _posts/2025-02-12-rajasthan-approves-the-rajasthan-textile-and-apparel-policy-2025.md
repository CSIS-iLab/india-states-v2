Rajasthan approves the Rajasthan Textile and Apparel Policy 2025. The policy aims to attract investments worth $4.57 billion over the next five years. Under the policy, the government will provide investment subsidies, employment boosters, tax reimbursements and electricity subsidies. Additionally, the government will provide support for micro, small, and medium enterprises too.
