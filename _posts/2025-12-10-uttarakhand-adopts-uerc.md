Uttarakhand adopts UERC (Tariff and Other Terms for Supply of Electricity from Renewable Energy Sources and Non-Fossil Fuel based Co-Generating Stations) (Second Amendment) Regulations, 2025. The Second Amendment, issued by the Uttarakhand Electricity Regulatory Commission (UERC) includes changes to the nomenclature of the principal regulations, formal additions of Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) definitions, and BESS life, depreciation, and tariﬀ norms.
