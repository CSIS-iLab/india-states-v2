Telangana issues the TGERC (Rooftop Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Grid-Interactive Systems) Regulations, 2025. Issued by the Telangana Electricity Regulatory Commission (TGERC), the regulations introduce a comprehensive framework governing metering for consumers in the state and will apply to distribution licensees, eligible consumers, and third-party owners of rooftop solar PV systems.
