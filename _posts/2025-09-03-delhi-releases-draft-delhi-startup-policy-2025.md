Delhi releases draft Delhi Startup Policy, 2025. The policy is designed facilitate the setting up of 5000 start-ups by 2035 by oﬀering financial incentives and reimbursements, and a single- window online clearance system for applying. Under the policy, the state will establish a $22.7 million (INR 200 crore) corpus for the Delhi Start-up Venture Capital Fund to provide financial access to these businesses.
