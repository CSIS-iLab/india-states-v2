Goa launches Pricing Policy for Innovative Lifesaving Therapies (PPILT). The policy aims at making next-generation treatments for cancer and complex diseases affordable and accessible. Under the PPILT, Goa will enter confidential, outcome- linked pricing agreements with medical device and pharmaceutical companies to bring treatments for conditions like cancer within the public healthcare budget and help alleviate the existing heavy costs away from patients.
