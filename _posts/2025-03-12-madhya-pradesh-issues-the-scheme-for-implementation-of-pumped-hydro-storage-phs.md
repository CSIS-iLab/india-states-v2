Madhya Pradesh issues the Scheme for Implementation of Pumped Hydro Storage (PHS) Project in Madhya Pradesh. The guidelines include methods to operationalize the incentives announced under the state’s renewable energy policy as well as modes of allotment of pumped storage sites to government and private sector developers. The policy will be valid for 10 years.
