Tamil Nadu passes the Money Lending Entities (Prevention of Coercive Actions) Bill, 2025, aimed at protecting vulnerable borrowers from unethical and aggressive debt recovery methods used by loan sharks. The bill criminalizes coercive recovery tactics and holds lenders accountable under abetment to suicide provisions if their actions lead to borrower suicides.
