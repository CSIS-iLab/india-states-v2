Madhya Pradesh amends Land Disposal Policy. Under the new rules, the reserved price of land will now be fixed at 100 percent of the area as per the District Collector’s guideline rate. As per the amendment to clause 10.11 of the Re-Densification Policy 2022, the oﬀset value, earlier determined based on 60 percent area and 100 percent guideline rate, will now be calculated on 100 percent area and 100 percent guideline rate.
