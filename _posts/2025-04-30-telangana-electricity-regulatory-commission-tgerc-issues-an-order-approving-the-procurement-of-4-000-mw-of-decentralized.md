Telangana Electricity Regulatory Commission (TGERC) issues an Order approving the procurement of 4,000 MW of decentralized, ground-mounted solar power under Component-A of the PM-KUSUM scheme. In response to the petition raised by the Northern and the Southern Telangana Power Distribution Companies, TGERC maintained the $0.037/kWh tariﬀ, citing procurement progress, and emphasized careful grid integration, consumer safeguards, and financial prudence in execution.
