Karnataka issues orders to implement the Supreme Court’s directive regarding a patient’s right to die with dignity becoming India’s second state, after Kerala, to implement this ruling. The orders directs the state health department to appoint medical experts to the secondary medical board to decide when life-sustaining treatment can be stopped for terminally ill patients.
