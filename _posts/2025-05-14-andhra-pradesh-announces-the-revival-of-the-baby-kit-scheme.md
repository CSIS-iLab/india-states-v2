Andhra Pradesh announces the revival of the Baby Kit scheme. Chief Minister N Chandrababu Naidu approved the Health Department’s proposal to provide essential items to newborns. The scheme which had previously received partial support from the National Health Mission (NHM) will now be fully funded by the state.
