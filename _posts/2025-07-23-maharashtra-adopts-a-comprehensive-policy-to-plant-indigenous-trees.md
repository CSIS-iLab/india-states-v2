Maharashtra adopts a comprehensive policy to Plant Indigenous Trees**. The new policy initiative by the Public Works Department aims to enhance ecological balance and combat climate change by planting indigenous trees along all national and state highways, major road projects, and rural thoroughfares. The policy also revises the funding model for tree plantation from Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) model to a new five distinct stage model where funds will be released only upon the successful completion of each phase.
