Andhra Pradesh approves Comprehensive Water Supply Improvement Scheme for the Mangalagiri-Tadepalli Municipal Corporation (MTMC). This initiative, led by the Municipal Administration and Urban Development (MA&UD) Department, aims to ensure reliable and equitable access to safe drinking water for all areas within MTMC’s jurisdiction until 2056.
