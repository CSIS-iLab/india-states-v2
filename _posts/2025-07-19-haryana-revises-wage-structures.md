Haryana revises Wage Structures. The state government as part of an attempt to standardise and enhance compensation for workers employed through the Haryana Kaushal Rozgar Nigam has introduced two salary slabs, one for employees earning monthly wage of INR 19,900 and the other for employees earning monthly wage of INR 24,100 to oﬀer more transparency and equitable remuneration for part-time and hourly workers.
