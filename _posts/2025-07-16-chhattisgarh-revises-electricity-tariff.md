Chhattisgarh revises Electricity Tariff. The tariﬀ order for FY 2025–26 approved by the Chhattisgarh Electricity Regulatory Commission (CSERC) increases the electricity tariﬀs for domestic and non-domestic consumers. To promote development in Left-Wing Extremism( LWE) aﬀected areas, the state government will oﬀer a 10 per cent rebate on electricity bills for mobile towers set up in these regions
