Sikkim launches the ‘Mukhyamantri Yuva Udhyamsheelta Vikas Yojna’ (Chief Minister Entrepreneurship Development Scheme). The scheme aims to promote self-employment in the state among young entrepreneurs. The state has given checks covering 50 percent of project costs up to a maximum of $5,723.5 (INR 500,000) to 132 entrepreneurs as grants.
