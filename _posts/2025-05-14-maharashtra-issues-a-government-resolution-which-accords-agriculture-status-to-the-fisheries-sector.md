Maharashtra issues a Government Resolution which accords agriculture status to the fisheries sector. Fish farmers and related stakeholders will now be eligible for various agricultural subsidies and schemes such as electricity concessions, Kisan Credit Cards, and bank loans at agricultural rates. Additionally, they will benefit from low-cost insurance and solar energy initiatives.
