Nagaland presents the State Budget, 2025-26 with an outlay of $2.85 billion. Projected gross receipts and expenditures stand at $2.84 billion and $2.832 billion respectively. The surplus of $17.2 million is expected to be used for public account expenses, including pensions and wages.
