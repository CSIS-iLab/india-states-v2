Karnataka introduces the Karnataka Public-Private Partnership (PPP) Policy, 2025, to tackle financial hurdles and enhance infrastructure development. The policy opens 109 sectors and sub-sectors for private investment, aiming to attract more participation from the private sector in the state's development projects.
