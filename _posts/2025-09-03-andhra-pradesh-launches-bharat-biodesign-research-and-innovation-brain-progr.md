Andhra Pradesh launches Bharat Biodesign Research and Innovation (BRAIN) program. The program, in collaboration with APAC Biodesign Alliance and Stanford Biodesign, aims to develop aﬀordable medical technologies and workforce training, with an initial focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI)-enabled healthcare.
