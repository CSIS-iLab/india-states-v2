Karnataka approves the North-South Corridor. Under this corridor a 16.7 km tunnel road will be built linking Esteem Mall at Hebbal Junction to Central Silk Board Junction in HSR Layout. After technical reviews are completed the state government will float a global tender either on a hybrid model or BOOT (build–own–operate– transfer) model.
