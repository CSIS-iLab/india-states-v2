Andhra Pradesh unveils draft Electronics Component Manufacturing Policy 4.0 (ECMP 4.0). The draft policy aims to establish Andhra Pradesh as the premier destination for the electronics industry by providing world-class infrastructure and a conducive policy environment, fostering innovation and emerging technologies, bringing the entire electronics value chain to the state, and creating large-scale employment opportunities.
