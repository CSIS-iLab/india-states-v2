Kerala releases the Foreign Liquor (5th Amendment) Rules, 2025 laying down the mechanism for issuing liquor serving licences to government and private IT parks. According to the amended rules, the licence for IT Parks will be issued for an annual fee of $11,710.9 under the name of the developer of the park.
