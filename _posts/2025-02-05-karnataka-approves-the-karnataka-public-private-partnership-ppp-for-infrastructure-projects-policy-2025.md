Karnataka approves the Karnataka Public Private Partnership (PPP) for Infrastructure Projects Policy, 2025. The policy involves asset monetisation, and institutes dedicated PPP cells in departments. The PPP cells will formulate long- term strategies and improve the implementation of PPP projects.
