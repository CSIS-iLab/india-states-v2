Andhra Pradesh approves reduction in Vacant Land Tax (VLT). A 50 per cent reduction in vacant land tax by the state government aims to incentivize building activity and ease financial pressure on property owners and developers across urban areas during the construction period. This measure is expected to improve project viability, encourage timely completion, and enhance land utilization across urban local bodies.
