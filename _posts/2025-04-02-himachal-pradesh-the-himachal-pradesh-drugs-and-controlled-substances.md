Himachal Pradesh the Himachal Pradesh Drugs and Controlled Substances (Prevention, De-Addiction and Rehabilitation) Bill, 2025, to address increasing drug abuse in the state. The bill includes provisions for life imprisonment for selling drugs to minors or near educational institutes, special court hearings for drug trafficking related cases, support for de-addiction treatment, and fines between $230-$580 for possession of smaller quantities of drugs.
