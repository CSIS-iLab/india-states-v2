West Bengal issues the WBERC (Grid Interactive Rooftop Solar Photovoltaic System for Prosumers) Regulations, 2025. Issued by the West Bengal Electricity Regulatory Commission (WBERC), the regulations apply to all grid-interactive rooftop solar systems installed by prosumers connected to distribution licensees.
