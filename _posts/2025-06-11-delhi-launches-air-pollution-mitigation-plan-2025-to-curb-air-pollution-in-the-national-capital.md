Delhi launches Air Pollution Mitigation Plan 2025 to curb air pollution in the national capital. The state government’s multi-pronged strategy has many initiatives including accelerating Delhi’s transition to electric mobility and bar on entry of older vehicles into the city using Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) cameras at all border entry points.
