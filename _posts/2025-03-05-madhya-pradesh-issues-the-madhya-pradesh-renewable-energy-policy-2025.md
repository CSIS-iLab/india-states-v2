Madhya Pradesh** issues the **Madhya Pradesh Renewable Energy Policy, 2025**, targeting 50 percent of annual power consumption from renewable energy resources by 2030. The policy will be valid for five years and apply to all renewable energy projects of at least 500 kW. It will exclude decentralized renewable energy systems.
