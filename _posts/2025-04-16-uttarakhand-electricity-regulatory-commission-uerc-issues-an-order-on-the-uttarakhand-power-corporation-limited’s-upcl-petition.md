Uttarakhand Electricity Regulatory Commission (UERC) issues an order on the Uttarakhand Power Corporation Limited’s(UPCL) petition seeking an additional surcharge from April to September 2025 to recover stranded fixed costs from consumers who opt for open access. The petition cites unutilized power purchase agreements under the Electricity Act, 2003.
