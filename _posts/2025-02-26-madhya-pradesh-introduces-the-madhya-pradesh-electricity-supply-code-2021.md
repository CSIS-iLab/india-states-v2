Madhya Pradesh introduces the Madhya Pradesh Electricity Supply Code 2021 (Fifth Amendment) \[ARG-1(II)(v) of 2025\]. The amendment includes key changes such as updating the definition of “applicant” to include individuals or groups who apply for an electricity supply, such as property owners, developers, and groups of consumers. Additionally, the definition of a “consumer” has been expanded to include individuals who receive electricity for personal use, either from a licensed supplier or the government.
