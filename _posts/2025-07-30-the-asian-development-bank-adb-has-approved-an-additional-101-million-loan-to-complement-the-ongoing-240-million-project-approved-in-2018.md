West Bengal expands the West Bengal Drinking Water Sector Improvement Project. The Asian Development Bank (ADB) has approved an additional $101 million loan to complement the ongoing $240 million project approved in 2018. The project focuses particularly on regions aﬀected by arsenic, fluoride, and salinity contamination, such as Purba Medinipur and South 24 Parganas districts.
