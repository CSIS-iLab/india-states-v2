Odisha amends the Odisha Semiconductor Manufacturing and Fabless Policy, 2023. The state government has made 16 significant changes in the policy including a special package for first few projects in each category, enhanced performance- linked incentives (PLI), relocation support for units shifting to Odisha, and incentives for semiconductor park developers. The amendment also strengthens the provisions for R&D and workforce skilling.
