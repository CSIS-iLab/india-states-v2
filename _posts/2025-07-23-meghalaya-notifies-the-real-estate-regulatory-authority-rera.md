Meghalaya notifies the Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA). The state government has established the RERA under the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 with an aim to promote fair practices, protect homebuyers' interests, resolve disputes efficiently, and boost investor confidence by streamlining project approvals and enforcing compliance.
