Haryana launches ‘Saheli' programme. The state health department launched this new initiative to prevent unsafe and illegal abortions. Under this initiative ASHA (Accredited Social Health Activists) workers make daily calls to pregnant women for real-time monitoring and prompt miscarriage reporting.
