Odisha launches the “Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana” and begins the distribution of central government’s “Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri-Jan Arogya Yojana” (AB-PMJAY) and the state’s “Gopabandhu Jan Arogya Yojana” unified health insurance cards. The scheme aims to cover 35 million people in the state. The Ayushman Vay Vandana Yojana will provide universal health coverage to all citizens aged 70+, regardless of socioeconomic status.
