Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) approves a pre-determined levelized tariﬀ of 3.04 (~$0.036)/kWh for 5,000 MW solar capacity under Component- A of the “Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Surakshaevam Utthaan Mahabhiyan” (PM- KUSUM). This tariﬀ will also be the ceiling price in future competitive bidding processes.
