Punjab approves The Seeds (Punjab Amendment) Bill, 2025. The bill ensures the sale of spurious seeds as a non-bailable oﬀence. It aims to ensure a steady supply of high-quality seeds to the farmers of the state. If a first oﬀense, the bill mandates a prison sentence of one to two years, along with a fine.
