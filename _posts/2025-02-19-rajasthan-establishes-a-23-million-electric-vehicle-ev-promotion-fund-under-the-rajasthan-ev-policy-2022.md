Rajasthan establishes a $23 million electric vehicle (EV) promotion fund under the Rajasthan EV Policy, 2022. The initiative, aligned with the Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Hybrid and EVs (FAME-II) Guidelines, aims to encourage the adoption of EVs by oﬀering state tax reimbursements and one-time grants to buyers of EVs equipped with modern battery technology
