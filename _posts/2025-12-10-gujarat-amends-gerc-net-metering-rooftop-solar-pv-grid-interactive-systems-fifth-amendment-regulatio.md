Gujara amends (GERC) Net Metering Rooftop Solar PV Grid Interactive Systems (Fifth Amendment) Regulations, 2016.The regulations issued by the Gujarat Electricity Regulatory Commission (GERC) introduce changes aimed at streamlining rooftop solar installations across the state. The amendment removes the requirement for a separate written connectivity agreement between consumers and distribution companies (DISCOMs) for systems installed under the ‘PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana’ (Prime Minister’s Solar Home Free Electricity Scheme).
