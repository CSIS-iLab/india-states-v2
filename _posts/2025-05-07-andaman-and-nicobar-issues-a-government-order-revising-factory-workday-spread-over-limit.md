Andaman and Nicobar issues a Government Order revising factory workday spread-over limit. The order aimed at improving the ease of doing business environment in the union territory, increases the daily spread-over limit for adult workers in factories from 10.5 hours to 12 hours, inclusive of rest intervals. This change, issued under Section 56 of the Factories Act, 1948, aligns with the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade’s March 2025 directive to ease compliance and enhance industrial productivity. The revised limit came into eﬀect on April 17, 2025.
