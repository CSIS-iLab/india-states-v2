Rajasthan approves the Rajasthan Data Centre Policy 2025 aimed at encouraging private sector data centre investments in the state. The policy has several features including asset creation incentives, sunrise incentives, interest subsidy, exemption in banking, transmission and wheeling charges, flexible payment facility related to land, exemption in stamp duty, land conversion and electricity charges and exemption from external development fee.
