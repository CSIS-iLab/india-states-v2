Mizoram unanimously passes a resolution to adopt the Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013 for application in the state. The state’s Mizoram Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2016 had been deemed inapplicable by the Guwahati High Court necessitating the adoption of the central government law.
