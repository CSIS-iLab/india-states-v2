Arunachal Pradesh approves Power Sector Revamp. The plan aims to modernize the electricity grid and ensure a more reliable and efficient power supply. The state is implementing smart metering systems to enhance operational efficiency, enable real-time monitoring, and oﬀer transparent and timely billing for consumers. Funds have been earmarked to also tackle aggregate technical and commercial (AT&C) losses that have historically impeded efficient power distribution in remote areas.
