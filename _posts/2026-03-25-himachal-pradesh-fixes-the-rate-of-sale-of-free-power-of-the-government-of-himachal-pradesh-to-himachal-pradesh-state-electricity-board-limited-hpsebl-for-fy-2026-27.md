Himachal Pradesh fixes the rate of sale of Free Power of the Government of Himachal Pradesh to Himachal Pradesh State Electricity Board Limited (HPSEBL) for FY 2026-27. The Himachal Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (HPERC) has set the rate at ~$0.0292 (INR 2.73)/kWh for the financial year, marking an increase from ~$0.0289 (2.70)/kWh in the previous year
