Andhra Pradesh launches a Price Support Scheme to procure Jowar at a Minimum Support Price (MSP) of $39.47 per quintal for the Rabi 2024–25 season. The nodal agency began procurement on April 20 and will continue until June 20, 2025, targeting an initial 10 percent of the estimated 2,77,080 metric tonnes of production. Depending on market conditions, procurement may scale up to 25 percent.Procurement will be carried out through government centres, with Aadhaar-based direct payment to farmers.
