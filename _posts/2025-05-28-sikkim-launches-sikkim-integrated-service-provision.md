Sikkim launches Sikkim Integrated Service Provision and Innovation for Reviving Economic Program (Sikkim-INSPIRES) action plan. Under this plan the state has prepared a new strategy to address mental health issues in technical collaboration with the Centre for Mental Health Law and Policy (CMHLP), Indian Law Society, Pune.
