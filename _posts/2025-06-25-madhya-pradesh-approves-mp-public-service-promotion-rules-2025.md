Madhya Pradesh approves MP Public Service Promotion Rules-2025. The rules approved by the state cabinet aim to encourage efficiency, with clear criteria defining ineligibility for promotion. The rules provide an opportunity to public servants of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes to get promotion on the basis of merit and also aim to create approximately 2 lakh new posts.
