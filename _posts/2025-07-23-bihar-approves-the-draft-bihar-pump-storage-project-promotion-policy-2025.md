Bihar approves the draft Bihar Pump Storage Project Promotion Policy, 2025. To attract investors the new policy oﬀers incentives such as exemption from water cess during the initial reservoir filling, concessions on intra-state transmission charges, no contribution towards Local Area Development Fund, and no demand of any free royalty power from these projects by the state government.
