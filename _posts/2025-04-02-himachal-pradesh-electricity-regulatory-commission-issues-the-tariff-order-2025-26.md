Himachal Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission issues the Tariff Order, 2025-26, reducing the electricity prices for domestic, commercial, and industrial consumers. Domestic consumers will get electricity $0.0017 cheaper per unit. Commercial consumers will see a $0.0014 per unit reduction. Small, medium, and large industries will benefit from a $0.002 per unit decrease to promote industrialization in the state. No changes have been made to fixed/demand charges for any category.
