Jharkhand issues draft JSERC ( Jharkhand State Electricity Regulatory Commission) Terms and Conditions for Determination of Generation Tariff Regulations, 2025.** The regulations set out rules for determining tariffs for electricity generation in the state. The regulations seek to promote efficiency, drive optimal investments, and enable fair cost recovery, while ensuring transparency and accountability in tariff determination.
