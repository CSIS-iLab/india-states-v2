Uttar Pradesh releases the Uttar Pradesh State Budget 2025-26 with an outlay of
$93.15 billion. The budget focuses on infrastructure, education, and agriculture. Key highlights include the revision of minimum monthly salaries for outsourced workers to $230.59 from $184.47. Additionally, the government announced free medical treatment up to $5764.8 for certain categories of government contractual employees.
