Mizoram presents the State Budget, 2025-26 with an outlay of $1.74 billion. The budget lays special emphasis on the implementation of the government’s flagship “Bana Kaih” Scheme** that aims to provide financial assistance to entrepreneurs and farmers, with an allocation of $40.13 million.
