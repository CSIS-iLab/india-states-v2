Mizoram teams up with Digital India BHASHINI Division (DIBD) of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. The Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between the state and central ministry aims to integrate BHASHINI’s advanced language technology platform into the Mizoram’s digital infrastructure, providing government services, information, and schemes in the Mizo language and other local dialects
