Telangana launches the “Rajiv Yuva Vikasam” Scheme with an allocation of $692 million to provide financial assistance to unemployed youth from scheduled castes, scheduled tribes, backward classes and minority communities. Under the scheme, the government will provide up to $3,450.03 assistance to 500,000 eligible beneficiaries through self-employment schemes.
