Haryana approves procurement of NPHC’s Firm and Dispatchable Renewable Energy (FDRE) Power. The petition filed by the Haryana Power Purchase Centre (HPPC) for procurement of 400 MW FDRE power from inter-state transmission system (ISTS) connected RE and energy storage projects was approved by the Haryana Electricity Regulatory Commission (HERC) at the tariﬀ of $0.051 per unit (₹4.44 per unit) for a period of 25 years.
