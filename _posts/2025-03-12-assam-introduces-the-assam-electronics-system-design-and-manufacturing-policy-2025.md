Assam introduces the Assam Electronics System Design and Manufacturing Policy, 2025. The policy oﬀers up to $26.7 million in incentives for entrepreneurs and aims to generate employment for 15,000 people in Assam. Additionally, the policy focuses on creating electronics manufacturing clusters, prioritizing sectors such as medical electronics, artificial intelligence, nano electronics, and robotics.
