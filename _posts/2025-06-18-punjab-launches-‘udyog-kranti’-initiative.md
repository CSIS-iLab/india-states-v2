Punjab launches ‘Udyog Kranti’ initiative. The state government launched this initiative to revolutionise the industrial growth. Under this initiative the state government has set up ‘Fast-track Punjab Portal’ to streamline all the approvals and introduced a deemed approval system under which all applications need to be processed within 45 days. If no decision is taken within the stipulated time the portal will automatically generate approval the next day.
