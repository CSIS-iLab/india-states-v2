Jharkhand State Electricity Regulatory Commission ( JSERC) increases the power tariﬀ in the state by 6.34 percent. Under the new rates, domestic consumers in rural areas will pay $0.079 per unit, up from $0.074, while urban consumers will pay $0.080 per unit, up from $0.078. The revised tariﬀ will come into eﬀect from May 1.
