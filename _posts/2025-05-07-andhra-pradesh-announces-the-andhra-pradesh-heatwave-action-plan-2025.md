Andhra Pradesh announces the Andhra Pradesh Heatwave Action Plan 2025 in the wake of the India Meteorological Department forecast, noting the possibility of excessive heatwaves and high temperatures continuing till June. The plan includes setting up cooling centres such as public buildings, schools, and malls, as well as temporary shelters for supporting people without adequate housing or access to water
