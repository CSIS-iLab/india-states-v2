Telangana releases the Telangana Tourism Policy 2025-2030. The policy aims to position Telangana among the top five states in domestic and international tourist arrivals, attract $1.74 billion of new investments in the tourism sector over the next five years, create additional employment opportunities for three lakh people and enhance tourism contribution to 10 percent or higher of the state GDP.
