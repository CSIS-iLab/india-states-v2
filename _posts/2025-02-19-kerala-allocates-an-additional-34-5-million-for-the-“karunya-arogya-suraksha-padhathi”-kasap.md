Kerala allocates an additional $34.5 million for the “Karunya Arogya Suraksha Padhathi” (KASAP), the state’s integrated health protection scheme designed to provide coverage to over 6.4 million poor and vulnerable people. With this additional allocation, the total amount earmarked for the flagship scheme in the present financial year has gone up to $112.67 million.
