Punjab approves amendments to its Land Pooling Policy-2025. The policy increases annual compensation for landowners from $346.7 to $578 per acre to be payable upon issuance of a letter of intent from the state to take possession of the land. The letter, oﬀering a 1,000sq yard residential plot and a 200sq yard commercial plot per acre of land, will be issued within 21 days. This compensation will be raised to $1,155 per acre annually after the government takes possession of the land.
