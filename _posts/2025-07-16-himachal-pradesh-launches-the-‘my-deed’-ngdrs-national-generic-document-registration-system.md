Himachal Pradesh launches the ‘My Deed’ NGDRS (National Generic Document Registration System). The Pilot Project launched by the state government aims to simplify and modernize the land registration process. Under this new system, citizens will now need to visit the tehsil office only once to complete the land registry process. Land registration can be applied online, anytime and from anywhere, saving time and eﬀort.
