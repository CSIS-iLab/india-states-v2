Goa extends the validity of the “Goa State Incentives to Encourage Investments Scheme, 2017” for an additional year, from April 1, 2025, to March 31, 2026. The scheme, also known as the “Umbrella Scheme,” provides financial assistance to enterprises in Goa to promote industrial growth and attract new investments.
