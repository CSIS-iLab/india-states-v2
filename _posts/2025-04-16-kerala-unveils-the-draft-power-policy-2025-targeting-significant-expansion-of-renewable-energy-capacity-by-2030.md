Kerala unveils the draft Power Policy 2025, targeting significant expansion of renewable energy capacity by 2030. The state plans to add 1 GW through utility-scale solar projects and solar parks, 2.5 GW via decentralized solar initiatives, and 1.5 GW from non-solar sources such as wind and bioenergy.
