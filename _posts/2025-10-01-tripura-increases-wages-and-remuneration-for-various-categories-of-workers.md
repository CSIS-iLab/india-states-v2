Tripura increases wages and remuneration for Various Categories of Workers. The categories include Daily Rated Workers (DRWs), Monthly Rated Workers (MRWs), contingent and casual workers, part-time workers, permanent labourers, employees in public places of worship, village ‘chowkidars’ (guards), school mothers, adult literacy teachers, part-time instructors, community health guides, and home guards. The revised rates will come into eﬀect from October 1, 2025.
