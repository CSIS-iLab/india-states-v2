Maharashtra approves the Maharashtra Code on Wages Rules, 2025, and the Maharashtra Industrial Relations Code Rules, 2025, aligning with the Central Government’s labour codes framework. These reforms, part of India's broader labour law consolidation, aim to simplify wage regulation and industrial relations. The rules will now be submitted to the Centre for final approval, marking a step toward uniform implementation across states.
