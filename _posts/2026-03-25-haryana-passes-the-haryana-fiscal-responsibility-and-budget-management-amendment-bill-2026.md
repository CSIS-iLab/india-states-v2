Haryana passes the Haryana Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (Amendment) Bill, 2026. The bill caps incremental guarantees on term loan to be given during a year at 5 percent of revenue receipts of the previous year or 0.5 percent of Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) of the previous year, whichever is lower.
