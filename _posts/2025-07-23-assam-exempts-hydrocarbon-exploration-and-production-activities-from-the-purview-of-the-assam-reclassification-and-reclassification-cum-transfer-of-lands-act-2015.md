Assam exempts hydrocarbon exploration and production activities from the purview of the Assam Reclassification and Reclassification-cum-Transfer of Lands Act, 2015.The move aims to ease land access for energy companies and fast-track strategic hydrocarbon projects bringing in more money for the state, improve the country’s energy security, and make it easier to do business.
