Haryana increases its Dearness Allowance (DA). The allowance applies to state government employees who are drawing their salaries as per the 5th Pay Commission. The revised DA rate has been increased from the existing 466 per cent to 474 percent of basic pay, with eﬀect from July 1, 2025.
