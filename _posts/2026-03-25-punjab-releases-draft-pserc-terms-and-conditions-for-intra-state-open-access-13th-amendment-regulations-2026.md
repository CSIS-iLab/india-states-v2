Punjab releases draft PSERC (Terms and Conditions for Intra-State Open Access) (13th Amendment) Regulations, 2026.The regulations issued by the Punjab State Electricity Regulatory Commission (PSERC) have a key focus on the treatment of banked energy for green energy consumers and renewable energy-based captive power users. Under the proposed amendment, banked energy can now be carried forward on a month-to-month basis until the end of the settlement period, which runs from June 1 to May 31 of the following year.
