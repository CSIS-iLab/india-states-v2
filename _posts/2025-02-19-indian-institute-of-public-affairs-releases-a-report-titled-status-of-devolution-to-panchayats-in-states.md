Indian Institute of Public Affairs releases a report titled Status of Devolution to Panchayats in States. As per the report, Karnataka has been ranked first in devolution of power to panchayats in terms of funds, functions and functionaries followed by Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh.
