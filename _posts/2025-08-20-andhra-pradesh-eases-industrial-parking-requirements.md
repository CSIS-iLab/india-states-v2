Andhra Pradesh eases industrial Parking Requirements. The state halves the parking mandate for industrial areas from 20 percent to 10 percent. For layouts approved by the Andhra Pradesh Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (APIIC), parking will be provided through common pooled facilities at the planning stage, eliminating the need for individual units to create separate parking if shared capacity is adequate.
