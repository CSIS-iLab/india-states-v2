Punjab releases the draft Green Hydrogen Policy aiming to achieve green hydrogen and ammonia production capacity of 100 kilo tonnes per annum by 2030. The policy proposes extending incentives under the existing “Punjab Industrial and Business Development Policy 2022” to new green hydrogen and ammonia ventures and expanding the existing units, with benefits varying by project category.
