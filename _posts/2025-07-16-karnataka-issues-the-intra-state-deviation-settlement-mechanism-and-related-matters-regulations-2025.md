Karnataka issues the Intra-State Deviation Settlement Mechanism and Related Matters Regulations, 2025. The new regulations issued by the Karnataka Electricity Regulatory Commission (KERC) establishes a commercial mechanism to ensure grid users’ adherence to scheduled electricity injection and drawal and maintain grid security and stability.
