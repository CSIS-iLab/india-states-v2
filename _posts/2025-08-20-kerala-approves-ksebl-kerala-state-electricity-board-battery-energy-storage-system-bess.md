Kerala approves KSEBL (Kerala State Electricity Board) Battery Energy Storage System (BESS). The proposal, under the KSERC (Kerala State Electricity Regulatory Commission), sets up a 125 MW/500 MWh battery energy storage system (BESS) at four substations: Sreekantapuram (40 MW/160 MWh), Mulleria (15 MW/60 MWh), Areacode (30 MW/120 MWh), and Pothencode (40 MW/160 MWh).
