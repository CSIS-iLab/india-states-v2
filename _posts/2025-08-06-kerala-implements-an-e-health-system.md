Kerala implements an e-health system. The system is active across 800 public health institutions as part of Kerala's digital health transformation eﬀorts. The system includes a digital payment system, online outpatient (OP) ticket booking, test results, and prescriptions. An objective of this new system is to reduce wait times in hospital queues.
