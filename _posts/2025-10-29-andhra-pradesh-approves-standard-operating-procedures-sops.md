title: Andhra Pradesh approves Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for online
  Change of Land Use (CLU) Permissions. The new SOP, under the Andhra Pradesh
  Development Permission Management System (APDPMS) 2.0 aims to enhance the ease
  of doing business across the state. The new framework enables individuals,
  developers, and institutions to apply online for land conversions, such as
  agricultural to residential or residential to commercial, through a fully
  digital, paperless process governed by a strict 45-day Service Level Agreement
  (SLA).
