Central Electricity Regulatory Commission (CERC) issues draft regulations to determine fees and charges to be collected by Regional Load Despatch Centres (RLDCs) from the generating companies, distribution companies (DISCOMs), bulk consumers, inter-state transmission licensees, buyers, sellers and inter-state trading licensees, settlement nodal agency and any other users.
