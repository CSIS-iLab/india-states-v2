Madhya Pradesh releases a Draft Fifth Amendment to the MPERC (Co-Generation and Generation of Electricity from Renewable Sources of Energy), (Revision-II), Regulations, 2021. The amendment, issued by the Madhya Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (MPERC) mandates obligated entities, including distribution companies (DISCOMs), open access consumers, and captive power users to adhere to a specific renewable purchase obligation (RPO).
