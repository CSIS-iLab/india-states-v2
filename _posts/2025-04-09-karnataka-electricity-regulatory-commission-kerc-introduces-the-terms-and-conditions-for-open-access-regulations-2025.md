Karnataka Electricity Regulatory Commission(KERC) introduces the Terms and Conditions for Open Access Regulations, 2025. The updated regulations aim to allow specific electricity consumers to use the state’s power transmission and distribution systems, improve accessibility to electricity and promote competition in the power sector.
