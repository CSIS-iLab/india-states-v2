Andhra Pradesh releases the operational guidelines for the Andhra Pradesh Industrial Development Policy (4.0) 2024–29, aimed at positioning the state as a competitive manufacturing hub. Key incentives include a capital subsidy of up to 15 percent of fixed capital investment (FCI), top-up subsidies for production linked incentives-approved projects (up to 5 percent of FCI), employment-linked subsidies of up to 10 percent of FCI, and 100 percent net state goods and services tax reimbursement for five years.
