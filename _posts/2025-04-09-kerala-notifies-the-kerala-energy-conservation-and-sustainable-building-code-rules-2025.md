Kerala notifies the Kerala Energy Conservation and Sustainable Building Code Rules, 2025. The rules outline the procedures to be followed during the design, construction, and completion phases of buildings being constructed in the state.Additionally, the rules outline the provisions for commercial building construction to ensure energy conservation and environmental sustainability under the Energy Conservation and Sustainable Building Code (ECSBC) published by Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE).
