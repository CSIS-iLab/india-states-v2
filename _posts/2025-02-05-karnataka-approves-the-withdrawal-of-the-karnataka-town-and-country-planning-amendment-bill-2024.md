Karnataka approves the withdrawal of the Karnataka Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Bill, 2024, which previously proposed the introduction of the premium floor area ratio (FAR). The move comes after the Governor returned the Bill, seeking clarifications on legal aspects, particularly its potential impact on transferable development rights (TDR) and urban planning norms.
