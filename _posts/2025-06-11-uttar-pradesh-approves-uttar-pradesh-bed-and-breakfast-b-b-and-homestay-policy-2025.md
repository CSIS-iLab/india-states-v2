Uttar Pradesh approves Uttar Pradesh Bed and Breakfast (B&B) and Homestay Policy-2025. Under the new policy, for a small fee, any individual residing in a proximity to a religious or tourist site will be allowed to register a homestay unit and oﬀer visitors lodging options given existing hotels are not able to meet the demand.
