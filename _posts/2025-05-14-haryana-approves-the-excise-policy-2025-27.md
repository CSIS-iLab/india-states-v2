Haryana approves the Excise Policy, 2025-27. As per the policy, the state will not permit sub-vends in villages with a population of 500 or less. Additionally, the state has imposed a blanket ban on all forms of liquor advertising—including within licensed premises—as part of its revised excise framework. The new policy prescribes significantly higher penalties for violations: $1,200 for the first oﬀence, $2,400 for the second, and $3,600 for the third.
