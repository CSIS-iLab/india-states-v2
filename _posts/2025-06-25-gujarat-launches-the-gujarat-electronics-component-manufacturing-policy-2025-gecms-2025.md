Gujarat launches the **Gujarat Electronics Component Manufacturing Policy-2025 (GECMS-2025). Under the new policy the state government will oﬀer 100 per cent top-up incentives for projects approved by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), oﬀering dual benefits from both the Centre and the state. The strategic target sectors are multi-layer and HDI printed circuit boards (PCBs), lithium-ion battery cells, specialised machinery etc.
