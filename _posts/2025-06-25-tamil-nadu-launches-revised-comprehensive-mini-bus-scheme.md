Tamil Nadu launches revised Comprehensive Mini-Bus Scheme. The revised scheme extends mini-bus services to 25,708 kilometres of unserved areas in the State. Key features of the scheme include an increased maximum route length of 25 km, with at least 65 per cent of the route required to cover areas currently underserved by regular bus services. The routes may also be extended to nearby public facilities such as libraries, hospitals, government offices, or schools if they fall within a one- kilometre radius of the route endpoint.
