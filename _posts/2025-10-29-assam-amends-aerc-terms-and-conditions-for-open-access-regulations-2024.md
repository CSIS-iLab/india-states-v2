Andhra Pradesh issues APERC (Terms and Conditions for Tariff Determination from Renewable Energy Sources) Regulation, 2025. The regulations, issued by the Andhra Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (APERC), lays down the framework for determining tariﬀs for renewable energy projects under Section 62 of the Electricity Act, 2003, and applies to all grid-connected renewable energy-based generating stations commissioned during the control period.
