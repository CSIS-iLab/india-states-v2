Andhra Pradesh simplifies Getting New Electricity Connections. The state regulator, Andhra Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (APERC), has fixed connection charges for new or additional loads up to 150 kW to streamline electricity connections for small and medium consumers. These charges will only apply when the service location lies within 1 km of an electrified network.
