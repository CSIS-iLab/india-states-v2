Odisha hikes daily wages for workers. New per day wages for unskilled workers $4.97 (INR 472), semi-skilled workers $5.49 (INR 522) and highly skilled workers $6.02 (INR 572) will be implemented retroactively from April 1<sup>st</sup>, 2026.
