Manipur adopts the Central Government’s Child Care Leave (CCL) Policy, extending benefits to women employees and single male employees with minor children below 18 years of age. Eligible employees can now avail up to 730 days of CCL during their service to care for up to two eldest surviving children. Additionally, the state introduces a Special Maternity Leave (SML) of 60 days for female employees in the case of stillbirth or the death of a child soon after birth, following the central guidelines.
