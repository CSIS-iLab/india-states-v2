Rajasthan approves tariﬀ for solar projects under ‘Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan (PM-KUSUM)’.The levelized tariﬀs determined for 78 solar projects through a competitive bidding process conducted by the Jaipur Vidyut Vitran Nigam ( JVVNL) was approved by the Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC). These projects with a total capacity of 228.85 MW will be implemented under Component C (feeder-level solarization) of PM-KUSUM.
