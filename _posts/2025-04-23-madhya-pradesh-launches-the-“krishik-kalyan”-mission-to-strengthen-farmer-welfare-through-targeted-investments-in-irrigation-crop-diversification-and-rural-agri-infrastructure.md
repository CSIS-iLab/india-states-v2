Madhya Pradesh launches the “Krishik Kalyan” Mission to strengthen farmer welfare through targeted investments in irrigation, crop diversification, and rural agri- infrastructure. The mission focuses on input support for smallholders, expansion of organic and millet farming, and localized value chains via FPOs and procurement networks.
