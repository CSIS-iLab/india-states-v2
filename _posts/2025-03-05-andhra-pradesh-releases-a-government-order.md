Andhra Pradesh releases a government order for the implementation of the “PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana” (PM Solar Rooftop Scheme). The guidelines aim to facilitate the implementation of the scheme for 2 million scheduled caste, and scheduled tribe citizens. The implementation guidelines are aligned with the state’s “Jagjeevan Jyothi Yojana”, which aims to provide free electricity to select consumers.
