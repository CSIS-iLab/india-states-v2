Uttar Pradesh launches Skill training for economically vulnerable families. The initiative launched under the state government’s Zero Poverty Campaign aims to provide skill training and assistance in securing jobs to the members of identified families and link economically vulnerable families with stable livelihood opportunities, ensuring a steady income and a better quality of life.
