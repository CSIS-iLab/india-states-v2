Tamil Nadu launches the Electronics Components Manufacturing Scheme to promote large-scale value-added manufacturing in the state. The scheme complements the Tamil Nadu Semiconductor and Advanced Electronics Policy, 2024 and aims to attract companies eligible under the central Electronics Component Manufacturing Scheme by matching Union-level subsidies. The state expects to mobilize $3.6 billion in investments and generate 60,000 jobs through this initiative.
