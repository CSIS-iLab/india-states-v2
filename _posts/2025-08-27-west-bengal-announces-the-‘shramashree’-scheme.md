West Bengal announces the ‘Shramashree’ Scheme. The scheme aims to support migrant workers returning to the state by oﬀering a monthly stipend of $57 for up to one year. It also provides access to other welfare benefits such as ration cards and free healthcare under the ‘Swastha Sathi’ scheme.
