Madhya Pradesh announces that the government will provide permanent electricity connection for farmers at $0.057. The initiative aims to incentivize farmers to move away from temporary connections. Additionally, farmers will only be required to pay 5 percent of the operational costs if they are running a 3 horsepower (HP) pump, and 10 percent if they operate a 5-7.5 HP pump for irrigation.
