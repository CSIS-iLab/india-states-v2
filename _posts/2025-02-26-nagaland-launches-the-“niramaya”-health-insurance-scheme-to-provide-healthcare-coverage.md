Nagaland launches the “Niramaya” Health Insurance Scheme to provide healthcare coverage for individuals with autism, cerebral palsy, intellectual disability, and multiple disabilities. The scheme up to $1,150 annually for hospitalization, therapy, corrective surgeries, and alternative treatments.
