Meghalaya releases Draft MSERC (Terms and Conditions for Determination of Tariff for Generation from Renewable Energy Sources) Regulations, 2025.The draft, issued by the Meghalaya State Electricity Regulatory Commission (MSERC), invites comments and suggestions. The proposed regulations aim to update the tariﬀ framework for renewable energy projects and apply to the determination of the tariﬀ for the supply of electricity from renewable energy sources to the distribution licensee operating within the state.
