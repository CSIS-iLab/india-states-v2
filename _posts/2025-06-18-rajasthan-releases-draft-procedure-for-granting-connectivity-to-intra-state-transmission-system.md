Rajasthan releases draft Procedure for Granting Connectivity to Intra-State Transmission System. The draft regulations released by Rajasthan Electricity Regulatory Commission (RERC) apply to the applications made for Grant of Connectivity to the Intra State transmission system of the State Transmission Utility / Transmission licensee for 33 kV voltage level and above.
