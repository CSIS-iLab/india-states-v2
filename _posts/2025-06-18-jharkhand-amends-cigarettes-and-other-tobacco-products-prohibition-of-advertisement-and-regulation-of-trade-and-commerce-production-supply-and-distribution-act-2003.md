Jharkhand amends Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Prohibition of Advertisement and Regulation of Trade and Commerce, Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 2003. A few notable changes brought about by the amendment are bars on the following: use of any tobacco products in public place, operation of hookah bars, sale of cigarettes and any other tobacco products to any persons below the age of 21 years and in an area within 100 meter of any educational institution, hospital, health institution, public office and court.
