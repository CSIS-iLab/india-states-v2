Bihar unifies Value Added Tax (VAT) on Natural Gas. The state government’s decision to make the tax rate equal on domestic and commercial sales of Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Piped Natural Gas (PNG) will provide relief to small consumers as GAIL and other City Gas Distribution companies will be able to provide CNG and PNG to consumers at cheaper rates by paying tax at the same rate.
