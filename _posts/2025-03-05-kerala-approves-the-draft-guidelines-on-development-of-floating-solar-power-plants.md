Kerala approves the draft Guidelines on Development of Floating Solar Power Plants to set up solar projects in reservoirs, backwaters, freshwater bodies formed due to mining and quarrying, and land found unusable for agriculture. The guidelines aim to support the state’s objective of achieving 3 GW solar renewable energy capacity additions in the next two years.
