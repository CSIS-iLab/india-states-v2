Bihar Electricity Regulatory Commission (BERC) releases the draft Renewable Purchase Obligation (RPO) Regulation, 202 outlining the RPO compliance and the renewable energy certificate (REC) framework for 2025. This draft regulation aims to ensure a structured approach to increasing renewable energy consumption in the state while aligning with national policies.
