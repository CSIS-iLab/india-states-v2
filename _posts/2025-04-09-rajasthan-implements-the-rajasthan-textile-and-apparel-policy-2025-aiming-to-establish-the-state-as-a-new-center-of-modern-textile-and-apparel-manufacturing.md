Rajasthan implements the Rajasthan Textile and Apparel Policy, 2025 aiming to establish the state as a new center of modern textile and apparel manufacturing. The policy provides up to 28 percent capital subsidies, 5 percent interest subvention on loans, and additional incentives for employment generation, regional development, and environmental sustainability projects.
