Karnataka passes the Greater Bengaluru Governance Act, 2024. According to the act, the Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) will cease to exist. The city will have a new apex body to play the role of a coordinator, and not more than seven city corporations within the Greater Bengaluru area, whose boundaries may extend beyond the current BBMP limits.
