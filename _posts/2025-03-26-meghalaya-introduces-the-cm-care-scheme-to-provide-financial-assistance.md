Meghalaya introduces the CM Care+ Scheme to provide financial assistance for high- end tertiary and critical care treatments that are not covered under the Meghalaya Health Insurance Scheme (MHIS). The Care+ Scheme aims to go beyond the $6,000 coverage limit set by the MHIS-PM Jan Arogya Yojana.
