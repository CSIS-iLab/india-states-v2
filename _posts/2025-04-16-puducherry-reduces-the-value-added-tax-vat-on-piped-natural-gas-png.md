Puducherry reduces the value added tax (VAT) on piped natural gas (PNG). VAT on domestic PNG has been slashed from 14.5 percent to 5 percent, and from 14.5 percent to 7.5 percent for commercial and industrial usage. The revised rates have been applied from April 1, 2025 and the distribution is expected to begin within the next 3– 6 months.
