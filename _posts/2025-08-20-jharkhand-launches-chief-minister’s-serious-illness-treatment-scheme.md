Jharkhand launches Chief Minister’s Serious Illness Treatment Scheme. The scheme will offer beneficiaries free treatment up to $17,139 (INR 15 lakh) under the ‘Abua’ (our) Health Security Scheme. The Abua Scheme provides free insurance and treatment to underprivileged families in Jharkhand.
