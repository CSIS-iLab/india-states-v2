Meghalaya presents the State Budget, 2025-26 with an outlay of $3.16 billion. The budget has a deficit of $225.9 million for the current fiscal, which is around 2.96% of the Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP). Excluding borrowings, the total receipts are estimated to be $2.93 billion.
