Odisha amends Odisha Apparel and Technical Textiles Policy, 2022. The amendments extend greater incentives to new industrial units in the sector through an enhanced employment cost subsidy. New industrial units will now be eligible for a monthly incentive of $79.36 (INR 7000) per female worker and $68.45 (INR 6000) per male worker for a period of five years. Earlier, the subsidy was fixed at $68.45 (INR 6000) for female workers and $57.04 (INR 5000) for male workers.
