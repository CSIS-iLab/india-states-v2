Rajasthan launches the Rajasthan Logistics Policy, 2025 aiming to cultivate an investment-friendly industrial environment in the state and establish it as a leading logistics hub. The policy oﬀers capital subsidies of up to 25 percent on eligible investments and interest subsidies of 7 percent on term loans for the development of logistics infrastructure.
