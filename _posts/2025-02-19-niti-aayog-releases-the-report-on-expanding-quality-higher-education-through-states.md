NITI Aayog releases the report on Expanding Quality Higher Education through States and State Public Universities. Among all states and union territories, Jammu and Kashmir spends most for higher education as a percentage of GDP at 8.11 percent, followed by Manipur (7.25 percent), Meghalaya (6.64 percent), and Tripura (6.19 percent). In contrast, Delhi (1.67 percent), Telangana (2 percent), and Karnataka (2.01 percent) allocate significantly less towards higher education.
