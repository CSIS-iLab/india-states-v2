Gujarat launches trading in USD-denominated BSE Sensex derivatives at the India International Exchange (India INX), located in the GIFT International Financial Services Centre (IFSC). The new product will allow global investors to trade and settle BSE Sensex derivatives in US Dollars, facilitating greater participation from international traders and investors.
