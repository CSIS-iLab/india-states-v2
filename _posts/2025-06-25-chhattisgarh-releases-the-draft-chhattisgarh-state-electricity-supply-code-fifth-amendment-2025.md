Chhattisgarh releases the Draft Chhattisgarh State Electricity Supply Code (Fifth Amendment), 2025. The amendments released by the Chhattisgarh State Electricity Regulatory Commission (CSERC) revises contract demand limits based on supply voltage and simplifies procedures for rooftop solar PV installation.
