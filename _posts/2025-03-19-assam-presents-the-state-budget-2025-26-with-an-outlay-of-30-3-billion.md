Assam presents the State Budget 2025-26 with an outlay of $30.3 billion. The budget reinstituted the provision for the sale of land between people from diﬀerent religions. Additionally, it focuses on the government’s land bank development and providing safeguards against land encroachment. This is the government last full budget before the state heads into legislative assembly elections in May 2026.
