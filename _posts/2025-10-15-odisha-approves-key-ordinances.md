Odisha approves Key Ordinances. The Odisha Jan Vishwas Ordinance, 2025 seeks to decriminalize minor oﬀences and promote trust-based governance, while the Shops and Commercial Establishment (Amendment) Ordinance, 2025 and the Factories (Odisha Amendment) Ordinance 2025, aim to simplify labor law compliance for small businesses and permit women to work night shifts across industry.
