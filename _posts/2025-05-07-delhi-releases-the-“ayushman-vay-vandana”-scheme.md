Delhi releases the “Ayushman Vay Vandana” Scheme, oﬀering free medical treatment worth up to $11,820 for citizens aged 70 years and above. Under this initiative, eligible seniors will receive free medical assistance of $5,910 annually. Additionally, $5,910 coverage will be provided under a separate Delhi government scheme, doubling the health protection to a total of $11,820.
