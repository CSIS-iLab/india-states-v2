Karnataka amends Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products Act (COTPA), 2003. The amended act Cigarettes and Other Tobacco Products (Prohibition of Advertisement and Regulation of Trade and Commerce, Production, Supply and Distribution) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 2024 puts a ban on sale of loose cigarettes or single sticks and hookah bars and prohibits sale of tobacco products within 100 metres of any educational institution.
