Andhra Pradesh introduces Bar Policy 2025-28. The new policy reforms the process of allotment of bar licenses. Replacing the earlier auction-based system of 2022-25, the state adopts a lottery-based mechanism to ensure fairness, accountability, and financial stability in the sector.
