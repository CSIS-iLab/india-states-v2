Odisha launches the ‘Shaktishri’ Programme. The initiative launched by the state government aims to ensure a safe and empowering environment for students and to prevent any form of harassment on campuses. The initiative will be implemented in 16 universities and 730 government and aided colleges under the state’s Higher Education Department.
