Jharkhand amends Renewable Energy TariffRegulations. The order issued by the Jharkhand State Electricity Regulatory Commission ( JSERC) titled “JSERC (Determination of Tariﬀ for Procurement of Power from Wind, Biogas, Municipal Solid Waste and Refuse Derived Fuel-based Power Projects) (Second Amendment) Regulations, 2025 extends the principal regulations of 2016 until March 31, 2030.
