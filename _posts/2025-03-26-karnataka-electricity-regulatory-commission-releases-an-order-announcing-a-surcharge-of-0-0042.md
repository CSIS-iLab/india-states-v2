Karnataka Electricity Regulatory Commission releases an order announcing a surcharge of $0.0042 per unit for all consumers except for the consumers receiving 200 free units of electricity under the Gruha JyothiScheme. This order also announces a surcharge of $0.004 per unit for 2026-27 and $0.0039 for 2027-28.
