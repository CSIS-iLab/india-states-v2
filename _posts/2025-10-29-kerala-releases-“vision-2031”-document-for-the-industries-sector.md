Uttarakhand mandates a Green Tax. The tax will be collected from vehicles coming to Uttarakhand from December 2025. The decision aims to control pollution, protect the environment, and promote cleanliness in the state. Diﬀerent tax rates have been fixed for various vehicle categories -- $0.91 (INR 80) for small vehicles, $2.84 (INR 250) for small cargo vehicles, $1.59 (INR 140) for buses, and between $1.36 (INR 120) and $7.96 (INR 700) for trucks.
