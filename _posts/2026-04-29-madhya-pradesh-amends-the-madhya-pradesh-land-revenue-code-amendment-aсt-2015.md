Madhya Pradesh amends The Madhya Pradesh Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Aсt, 2015 to increase land acquisition compensation. The state cabinet increased the compensation for farmers whose land is acquired for development projects. Under the revised policy, compensation for land acquired in rural areas will be increased up to four times the market value.
