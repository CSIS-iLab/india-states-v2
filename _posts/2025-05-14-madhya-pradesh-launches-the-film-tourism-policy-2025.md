Madhya Pradesh launches the Film Tourism Policy, 2025. As per the policy, the state will provide grants up to $600,000 for feature films, $240,000 for web series, $180,000 for TV serials, $60,000 for documentaries, $1.44 million for international films, and $24,000 for short films—provided 75 percent of production occurs in the state.
