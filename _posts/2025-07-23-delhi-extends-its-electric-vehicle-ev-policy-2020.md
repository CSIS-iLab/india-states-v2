Delhi extends its Electric Vehicle (EV) Policy, 2020. The state government has extended its EV policy by four months, pushing the new deadline to November 15, as a draft of the new policy is still under review and awaiting approval. Delhi’s EV Policy 2020 lapsed in 2023 but has been extended several times since.
