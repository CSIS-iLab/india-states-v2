Andhra Pradesh Electricity Regulatory Commission (APERC) introduces the Proposed Amendments in Regulations due to the Andhra Pradesh Integrated Clean Energy (ICE) Policy 4.0 amending the 2023 regulations concerning grid- interactive solar rooftop photovoltaic systems under net and gross metering. The original regulation has been amended to cover several key aspects, including provisions for distribution companies to work with distributed energy resource aggregators and the introduction of an application fee structure for solar rooftop systems.
